Item Shipment Preconditions

Item Shipment Preconditions can be entered on Items and Item Categories as either confirmations (e.g. allow for transport of dangerous goods, valid age for driving trucks) or messages (e.g., where to find information in the truck). Once defined, the shipment preconditions are shown on rental shipment of the item.

If a precondition has been defined for a confirmation, a Name or Name/Date of Birth must be entered before the shipment can be posted. Precondition messages appear prior to posting but require no action.

Note

If the preconditions are missing or do not meet minimum requirements, the shipment can continue with a confirmation message.
