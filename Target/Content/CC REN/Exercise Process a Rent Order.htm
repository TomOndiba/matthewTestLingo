Exercise – Process a Rent Order with Rate Components

On January 31, 2019, customer R-20000 requests one unit of item R-20000-01 for the period of 02/01/19 through 02/28/19. This item includes two rate parts (taxes and insurance) and a one-time cleaning fee due at the end of the rental.

Create and Ship the Rent Order

Follow these steps to create and ship the rent order:

  1. Set the Work Date to 01/31/19.
  2. In the navigation pane, click cc|rental > Contract Management > Rent Orders.
  3. Insert a new rent order for customer R-20000.
  4. Expand the Rental FastTab and enter the following:
    1. Rental Rate Code = Month
    2. Rental Start Date = 02/01/19
    3. Rental End Date = 02/28/19
    4. Prepaid = Yes
  5. In the Lines FastTab, select Item in the Type field.
  6. In the No. field, enter R-20000-01 and then press ENTER. Notice the following:
    • Three lines are added below the rent item line:
      • Two Rate Part line (INSURANCE and TAX1)
      • One-Time Invoicing line (CLEANRETURN)
    • Rental information on the rent item line (except for the Rate Unit Price) is the same as on the Rate Part lines, but is not carried to the One-Time Invoicing line

: Rent Order with rate component lines

  1. On the rent item line, enter 1 in the Quantity field. Notice that the same quantity is added to the Rate Component lines.
  1. In the Rate Unit Price field, enter the following values (if missing):
    • Rent item line  = 1375
    • Insurance line = 50
    • Tax line = 80

: Rent Order with rate unit prices added

  1. Select the rent item line and then click Line > Item Tracking Lines on the Lines FastTab.
    A dialog opens for item tracking selection.

: Shipment selection for item tracking

  1. With the Shipment option selected, click OK.
  2. On the ribbon, click Select Entries.

: Serial Number selection

  1. Note which serial number is selected and then click OK.
  2. Click OK to close the Item Tracking Lines page.
  3. On the ribbon, click Post.
  4. With the Ship option selected, click OK.

Invoice the Rent Order

Follow these steps to invoice the rent order:

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. Select the Invoice option and then click OK.
  3. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  4. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 02/28/19.
  5. Click OK and accept all confirmation messages.
  6. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  7. Double-click the line to open the Service Invoice Rental page. Notice that the rental item and the two Rate Part components appear with the same values entered on the Rent Order line.

: Service Invoice for rental and rate part component positions

  1. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  2. Escape back to the Rent Order.
  3. On the Navigate tab, click Entries > Rental Ledger Entries. Notice that the rental and rate part component lines appear.

: Rental Ledger Entries for a posted invoice with rental and rate part component positions

  1. Escape back to the Rent Order.

Return the Rent Order and Invoice the One-Time Invoicing Component

Follow these steps to return the rent order and invoice the One-Time Invoicing component:

  1. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/28/19.
  2. On the rental item line, enter 02/28/19 in the Rental Return Date field.
  3. In the Return Qty. to Receive field, enter 1. Notice that the Rate Part component lines are updated with the return information, but the One-Time Invoicing component line is still unchanged.

: Rent Order lines ready for return processing

  1. Select the rental item line and then click Line > Item Tracking Lines on the Lines FastTab.
  2. Select the Receive option and then click OK.

: Receipt selection for item tracking

  1. Confirm that the serial number is correct and then click OK to close the Item Tracking Lines page.

: Serial number to be returned

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. Select the Receive option and then click OK.
  3. Notice the following:
    • The receipt is processed on the rent item line and the rate part components.
    • The Quantity Shipped field and Qty. to Invoice fields are set to 1 on the One-Time Invoicing component line.

: Rent Order after return processing

  1. On the ribbon, click Reopen.
  2. On the One-Time Invoicing component line, type 100 in the Unit Price Excl. VAT field.
  3. On the ribbon, click Post.
  4. Select the Invoice option and then click OK.
  5. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/28/19.
  6. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 02/28/19.
  7. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  8. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  9. Open the invoice and notice that the cleaning charge appears on the line with the amount entered in step 11 as the unit price.

: Sales invoice for the One-Time Invoicing component

  1. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  2. Escape back to the rent order. Notice that the Quantity Invoiced field on the One-Time Invoicing component line is set to 1.
  3. On the Navigate tab, click Entries > Rental Ledger Entries. Notice that the One-Time Invoicing component line appears.

: Rental Ledger Entries for posted invoices with rental and all component positions

  1. Escape back to the rent order.
