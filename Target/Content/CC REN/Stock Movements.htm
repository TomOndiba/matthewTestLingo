Stock Movements

Items on Rent Orders that have not yet been shipped or returned can be viewed in the Rental Shipments and Rental Returns pages, accessed from the cc|rental menu by clicking Stock Movements > Shipments or Return Orders.

: Rental Shipments and Rental Returns pages

These pages provide a variety of information about the rental items to be shipped and returned. The Rent Orders can be opened from these pages, as well as any related service orders. Additionally, internal and external service orders can be created and counters can be updated.

The information on these pages is most useful for employees who handle the shipping and receiving processes, not necessarily the employees who are involved in quoting, invoicing or planning rentals.
