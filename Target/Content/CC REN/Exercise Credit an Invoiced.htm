Exercise – Credit an Invoiced Rent Order

Because of a prior billing arrangement, you must credit the invoice sent to customer R-30000 on April 1, 2019. You use the “Copy Document” function to complete this task.

Note

"Exercise – Extend a Rental Period" on page 1 must be completed to begin this exercise.

Follow these steps to create the credit memo:

  1. Set the work date to 04/01/19.
  2. In the navigation pane, click cc|rental > Contract Management > Credit Memos.
  3. On the ribbon, click New and then press ENTER to automatically populate the No. field.
  4. On the ribbon, click Actions > Copy Document.
  5. In the Document No. field, click the arrow, and use the “Choose Columns” function to show the Posting Date field.
  6. Click OK to close the message and then close the Posted Rental Invoices page.
  7. In the Document No. field, click the arrow, select the invoice posted to customer R-30000 on 4/1/2019 and then click OK.
  8. Select the Include Header check box.

: Copy Document request page

  1. Click OK.

: Credit memo ready to be posted

  1. On the ribbon, click Home > Post and then click Yes to post the credit memo.
  2. In the navigation pane, click Contract Management > Rent Orders.
  3. Open the rent order created in “Exercise – Extend a Rental Period” on page 1.
  4. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Entries > Rental Ledger Entries.

: Posted credit rental ledger entries

  1. Escape back to the rent order.
