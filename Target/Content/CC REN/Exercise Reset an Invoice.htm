Exercise – Reset an Invoice Period

Now that the credit memo has been posted against the rent order for customer R-30000, you must re-invoice it for the same period of April 1, 2019 through May 31, 2019.

Note

"Exercise – Extend a Rental Period" on page 1 and “Exercise – Credit an Invoiced Rent Order” on page 1 must be completed to begin this exercise.

Reset the Invoice Period

Follow these steps to reset the invoice period on the rent order:

  1. Open the rent order created in “Exercise – Extend a Rental Period” on page 1.
  2. Use the “Choose Columns” function to show the Next Invoice Period field on the lines. Notice that the field is empty.
  3. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Reset Invoice Period.
  4. Click Yes to reset the invoice period.
  5. In the Set New Invoice Period page, notice that the Start Date and End Date fields are empty. This is because the order had been fully processed.
  6. In the Start Date field, enter 04/01/19.
  7. In the End Date field, enter 05/31/19.
  8. Click OK.
  9. Notice that the Next Invoice Period field is now populated with the 04/01/19 – 05/31/19 date range as entered in the request page.

: Rent Order ready for re-invoicing

Re-invoice the Rent Order

Follow these steps to re-invoice the rent order:

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  3. In the Posting Date field, enter 04/01/19.
  4. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 05/31/19.
  5. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  6. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  7. Open the invoice and notice that the invoice is to be posted to the prepaid account for the two month invoice range.

: Invoice re-created for the 04/01/19 – 05/31/19 period

  1. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  2. Escape back to the rent order.
  3. On the Navigate tab, click Entries > Rental Ledger Entries.

: New invoice lines for the 4/1/2019 – 5/31/2019 period

  1. Escape back to the rent order.
