Rental Revenue Accounts

The G/L Accounts that are needed for cc|rental-related postings must be set up with the appropriate Business Posting Group and Product Posting Group combination(s).

: General Posting Setup page with rental accounts

The cc|rental-specific revenue account fields are described in the following table.

Field

Description

Internal Service Cost Account

Used for the posting reallocation of internal service order amounts that are posted to customer accounts (refer to topic “Solve Internal Service Costs Batch Job” on page 1).

Rental Account

Used for the posting of direct rental revenues which are not prepaid rental positions.

Rental Prepayment Account

Used for the posting of prepaid rental positions.

Rental Cr. Memo Account

Used for all positions to be credited from a Rent Order.

3rd Party Rent Account

Used for the posting of a purchase order that relates to a 3rd Party rental line (refer to topic “3rd Party Rental” on page 1).

Rent Line Disc. Account

Used for the posting of line discounts on a rental position.

Rent Inv. Disc. Account

Used for the posting of invoice discounts on a Rent Order.

Leasing Account

Used for the posting of a rental position with a Leasing Agreement (refer to topic “Leasing Agreement” on page 1).

Leasing Prepayment Account

Used for the posting of a prepaid rental position with a Leasing Agreement (refer to topic “Leasing Agreement” on page 1).

Rent Deposit Account

Used for the posting of refundable deposits on a Rent Order (refer to topic XXX).
