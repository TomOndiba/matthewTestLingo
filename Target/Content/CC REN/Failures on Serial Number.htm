Failures on Serial Number Tracked Items

Items that use serial number tracking must be assigned to the proper serial numbers to be credited. The serial numbers for failures can be selected from active serial numbers (shipped, not returned).

Failures are processed the same way for serial numbers as without, meaning based on whether the failure is within or outside of the invoicing period. The only difference is how the serial number is added to the failure.

Exercise – Failures with Serial Numbers within the Invoicing Period

Customer R-20000 orders three trucks (item R-20000-01, serial numbers NTGS35-00001 – NTGS35-00003) for the period of January 1, 2019 through March 31, 2019 at a price of 1375.00 each. This is a monthly invoicing agreement; however the customer has asked to be invoiced for January and February immediately.

On March 27th, the customer calls to say that one of the trucks has stopped running (NTGS35-00002). They will return it on March 31st and they do not want to be billed for the remainder of the contract for this truck.

Create, Ship, and Invoice the Rent Order

Follow these steps to create the rent order:

  1. Insert a new rent order for customer R-20000.
  2. Expand the Rental FastTab and enter the following:
    1. Rental Rate Code = Month
    2. Rental Start Date = 01/01/19
    3. Rental End Date = 03/31/19
    4. Month Based = Yes
  3. On the first line of the Lines FastTab, enter the following:
    1. Type = Item
    2. No. = R-20000-01
    3. Quantity = 3
    4. Rate Unit Price = 1375.00 (and add values if missing on Rate Component lines)
  4. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Item Tracking Lines.
  5. With the Shipment option selected, click OK.
  6. On the ribbon, click Select Entries and notice that NTGS35-00001 – NTGS35-00003 are selected.

: Select Entries page

  1. Click OK to close the Select Entries page.

: Serial numbers selected for the rental item

  1. Click OK to close the Item Tracking Lines page.
  2. On the ribbon, click Post.
  3. With the Ship option selected, click OK.
  4. On the ribbon, click Post.
  5. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  6. In the Posting Date field, enter 01/01/19.
  7. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 02/28/19.
  8. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  9. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  10. Open the invoice and then on the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  11. Escape back to the rent order.

Create the Failure

Follow these steps to create the failure:

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Enter Failure.
  2. Click Yes to create the failure period.
  3. In the Start Date Failure field, enter 03/27/19.
  4. In the End Date Failure field, enter 03/31/19.
  5. In the Failure Type field, enter DAMAGE.
  6. In the Quantity field, enter 1.
  7. On the ribbon, click Release. The following message appears:

: Serial number error message

  1. Click OK.
  2. On the ribbon, click Serial No. Selection. The following page appears:

: Serial No. Selection page

  1. Select the Select check box for the following serial numbers:
    • NTGS35-00001
    • NTGS35-00002
  2. Click OK to assign the serial numbers to failure.
  3. On the ribbon, click Release. The following message appears because too many serial numbers were selected:

: Serial number error message

  1. Click OK.
  2. On the ribbon, click Serial No. Selection.
  3. Clear the Select check box on the line for NTGS35-00001 (so that only NTGS35-00002 is selected) and then click OK.

: Only one serial number selected for failure processing

  1. On the ribbon, click Release.
  2. Click OK to close the Rental Failure Card page.

Process the Invoice with the Failure

Follow these steps to process the invoice with the failure:

  1. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  2. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  3. In the Posting Date field, enter 03/01/19.
  4. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  5. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  6. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  7. Open the invoice and notice the following:
    • A negative credit line for the failed unit from 03/27/19 – 03/31/19 appears on the invoice.
    • The credit is correctly calculated for the damaged unit (1375 rate unit price / 31 days * 5 days = 221.77).

: Failure assigned to the rental invoice (with rate component lines hidden)

  1. Select the failure line and then on the Lines FastTab, click Line > Item Tracking Lines.

: Serial number assigned to the failure

  1. Close the Serial Nos. Rental Document page.
  2. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  3. Escape back to the Rent Order.
  4. On the Navigate tab, click Entries > Rental Ledger Entries.
  5. Notice that the credit line appears for the proper amount, in the proper date range, for the proper quantity, for the proper serial number.

: Rental Ledger Entries including failure credit (with rate component lines hidden)

  1. Escape back to the rent order.
  2. Click the value in the Qty. Failures field.
  3. In the Rental Failures page, notice that the failure invoice was created through 03/31/2019.
  4. Escape back to the rent order.

Process the Return

Follow these steps to process the return:

  1. In the Posting Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  2. On the Lines FastTab, enter 03/31/19 in the Rental Return Date field.
  3. In the Return Qty. to Receive field, enter 3.
  4. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Item Tracking Lines.
  5. Select the Receive option and then click OK.
  6. Ensure that serial numbers NTGS35-00001 – NTGS35-00003 are selected and then click OK to close the Item Tracking Lines page.
  7. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  8. Select the Receive option and then click OK.
  9. Notice that the Return Qty. Received field is 3.

Exercise – Failures with Serial Numbers before the Invoicing Period

Customer R-20000 orders two trucks (item R-20000-01, serial numbers NTGS35-00001 – NTGS35-00002) for the period of January 1, 2019 through March 31, 2019 at a price of 1375 each. This is a monthly invoicing agreement and the January invoice is posted immediately.

In mid-January, the customer calls to say that one of the trucks is not running properly (NTGS35-00002). You remember that there was a repairable issue with this truck so you assure them that it can be fixed at no cost for the down period of January 15th through January 16th and tell them that they will receive a credit memo with the next invoice billing.

Create, Ship, and Invoice the Rent Order

Follow these steps to create the rent order:

  1. Insert a new rent order for customer R-20000.
  2. Expand the Rental FastTab and enter the following:
    1. Rental Rate Code = Month
    2. Rental Start Date = 01/01/19
    3. Rental End Date = 03/31/19
    4. Month Based = Yes
  3. On the first line of the Lines FastTab, enter the following:
    1. Type = Item
    2. No. = R-20000-01
    3. Quantity = 2
    4. Rate Unit Price = 1375.00 (and add values if missing on Rate Component lines)
  4. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Item Tracking Lines.
  5. With the Shipment option selected, click OK.
  6. On the ribbon, click Select Entries.
  7. Confirm that NTGS35-00001 and NTGS35-00002 are selected and then click OK.
  8. Click OK to close the Item Tracking Lines page.
  9. On the ribbon, click Post.
  10. With the Ship option selected, click OK.
  11. On the ribbon, click Post.
  12. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  13. In the Posting Date field, enter 01/01/19.
  14. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 01/31/19.
  15. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  16. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  17. Open the invoice and then on the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  18. Escape back to the rent order.

Create the Failure

Follow these steps to create the failure:

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Enter Failure.
  2. Click Yes to create the failure period.
  3. In the Start Date Failure field, enter 01/15/19.
  4. In the End Date Failure field, enter 01/16/19.
  5. In the Failure Type field, enter DAMAGE.
  6. In the Quantity field, enter 1.
  7. On the ribbon, click Serial No. Selection.
  8. Select the Select check box on the line for NTGS35-00002.

: Serial No. Selection page

  1. Click OK to close the Serial No. Selection page.
  2. On the ribbon, click Release.
  3. Click OK to close the Rental Failure Card page.

Process the Invoice with the Failure

Follow these steps to process the invoice with the failure:

  1. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  2. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  3. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  4. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 02/28/19.
  5. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  6. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  7. Open the invoice and notice that no credit entries appear.
  8. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  9. Escape back to the rent order.
  10. On the Navigate tab, click Credit Memos.
  11. Open the credit memo and notice that the credit is correctly calculated by the number of days for the truck (1375 rate unit price / 31 days * 2 days = 88.71).

: Failure credit memo

  1. Select the failure line and then on the Lines FastTab, click Line > Item Tracking Lines.

: Serial number assigned to the failure

  1. Notice that serial number NTGS35-00002 appears and then close the Serial Nos Rental Document page.
  2. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the credit memo.
  3. Escape back to the rent order.
  4. On the Navigate tab, click Entries > Rental Ledger Entries.
  5. Notice that there is a credit memo entry for the proper amount, in the proper date range, for the proper serial number.

: Rental Ledger Entries including failure credit

  1. Escape back to the rent order.
  2. Click the value in the Qty. Failures field and notice that the failure invoice/credit memo was created through 01/16/2019.
  3. Escape back to the rent order.

Process the Final Invoice and Return

Follow these steps to process the final invoice and complete the return:

  1. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  2. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  3. In the Posting Date field, enter 03/01/19.
  4. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  5. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  6. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  7. Open the invoice and then on the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  8. Escape back to the rent order.
  9. In the Posting Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  10. On the Lines FastTab, enter 03/31/19 in the Rental Return Date field.
  11. In the Return Qty. to Receive field, enter 2.
  12. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Item Tracking Lines.
  13. Select the Receive option and then click OK.
  14. Ensure that serial numbers NTGS35-00001 and NTGS35-00002 are selected and then click OK to close the Item Tracking Lines page.
  15. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  16. Select the Receive option and then click OK.
  17. Notice that the Return Qty. Received field is 2.
