Update Rental Contract Prices

The “Update Rental Contract Prices” batch job is used to update prices on rental contracts that need price updates. This batch job can also be used to print a test report.

: Update Rental Contract Prices batch job

When the batch job is run to update contract prices, the program updates the contract value of rental items as specified and recalculates relevant amounts on the rental contract and on the rental items included.

The fields on the Options FastTab are described in the following table.

Field

Description

Update To Date

Date up to which the prices are to be updated. The batch job will include contracts with next price update dates on or before this date.

Price Update %

Price update for the rental item contract values in percentages.

Action

Determines the action that occurs when the batch job is run:

  • Update Contract Prices – Performs the price update.
  • Print Only – Prints a test report based on the selected criteria. This report shows the contract numbers, customer numbers, contract amounts, price update percentages and any errors that occur.

The price updates depend on the formula in the Price Update Period field of the Service Contract Template, which is assigned in the Rental Mgt. Setup page. This information is automatically transferred to the Next Price Update Date and Price Update Period fields on the rent order line.

The date in the Next Price Update Date field on the rent order line must be reached before a change can be made to a price using this batch job. This date can be adjusted manually by entering a new date or changing the date formula in the Price Update Period field. Both of these fields, and the Last Price Update Date and Last Price Update % fields, must be displayed using the “Choose Columns” function.

After running the function, the Last Price Update Date and Last Price Update % fields on the lines display the updated values.

The Rental Line Price History page displays all price changes that have been made, by running the batch job or by manually changing the price on the line after shipping, but prior to invoicing. Manual changes to line discount percentages are also stored in this page.

The values in this page are also used to recalculate the total price on the rent order.

: Rental Line Price History with manual line discount and price change prior to invoicing

Based on the above criteria, calculations are made as follows:

  • If no invoicing has occurred, then the total rental time is recalculated.
  • If a portion of a rent order has been invoiced, the system uses the price validity periods to calculate the total price.

For example, in a year-long contract, if two months were invoiced each at 100.00 and three months each at 120.00, and the new price is 130.00, then the system calculates the remaining seven months each at 130.00; there is no pricing adjustment to the previously invoiced periods, even if the invoices are un-posted.

Exercise – Run the Update Rental Contract Prices Batch Job

On December 31, 2018, you receive a prepaid rent order from customer R-30000 for the 2019 time frame, to be paid in half year periods. As you create the order, you inform the customer that as of January 1, 2019, a 5% rate increase becomes effective, and that on July 1, 2019, a 2.5% rate increase will also occur. The customer accepts these terms and you process the orders after the accounting manager runs the “Update Rental Contract Prices” batch job as of January 1st and July 1st.

Create and Ship the Prepaid Rent Order

Follow these steps to create and ship the rent order:

  1. Set the Work Date to 12/31/18.
  2. Insert a new rent order for customer R-30000.
  3. On the Lines FastTab, use the “Choose Columns” function to show the Next Price Update Date, Price Update Period, Last Price Update Date, and Last Price Update % fields.
  4. In the lines, enter the following:
    1. Type = Item
    2. No. = R-30000-01
    3. Quantity = 1
    4. Rental Rate Code = Month
    5. Invoice Period = Half Year
    6. Prepaid = Yes
    7. Rental Start Date = 01/01/19
    8. Rental End Date = 12/31/19
    9. Price Update Period = 6M
    10. Next Price Update Date = 01/01/19
  5. Notice that the:
    • Rate Unit Price field is 350.00
    • Unit Price Total Rental Time and Amount Total Rental Time fields are 4200.00 (350 rate * 12 months)

 

: Rent order with pricing update fields

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. With the Ship option selected, click OK.
  3. Close the Rent Order.

Run the Update Rental Contract Prices Batch Job

Follow these steps to run the batch job:

  1. In the navigation pane, under cc|rental, click Periodic Activities > Update Rental Contract Prices.
  2. In the Update To Date field, enter 01/01/19.
  3. In the Price Update % field, enter 5.
  4. Click OK to run the batch job.
  5. Open the previously created rent order and notice that the:
    • Rate Unit Price field is 367.50 (350 old rate * 5% = 17.5)
    • Unit Price Total Rental Time and Amount Total Rental Time fields are 4410.00 (367.50 new rate * 12 months)
    • Next Price Update Date field is 7/1/2019, based on the date calculation in the Price Update Period field
    • Last Price Update Date and Last Price Update % fields show the updated information

: Updated rental prices

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Show Price History.

: Pricing update in the Rental Line Price History page

  1. Escape back to the rent order.

Invoice the Order

Follow these steps to invoice the order:

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. Select the Invoice option and then click OK.
  3. In the Posting Date field enter 01/01/19.
  4. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 06/30/19.
  5. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  6. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  7. Open the invoice and notice that the line, totaling 2205.00 (4410.00 invoice total / 2 for the half year), is to be posted to the Rental Prepayment Account (5792).

: Invoice for rent order with updated pricing

  1. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  2. Close the open pages.

Run the Update Rental Contract Prices Batch Job

Follow these steps to run the batch job:

  1. Change the Work Date to 06/30/19.
  2. In the navigation pane, under cc|rental, click Periodic Activities > Update Rental Contract Prices.
  3. In the Update To Date field, enter 07/01/19.
  4. In the Price Update % field, enter 2.5.
  5. Click OK.
  1. Open the previously created rent order and notice that the:
    • Rate Unit Price field is 376.69 (367.50 old rate + 2.5%)
    • Unit Price Total Rental Time and Amount Total Rental Time fields are 4465.14 (Six months at old rate of 367.50 (2205.00) + Six months at new rate of 376.69 (2260.14))
    • Next Price Update Date field is 1/1/2020, based on the date calculation in the Price Update Period field
    • Last Price Update Date and Last Price Update % fields show the latest update information

: Invoice for rent order with updated pricing

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Show Price History.

: Updated price history
