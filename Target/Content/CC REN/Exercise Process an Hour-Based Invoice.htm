Exercise – Process an Hour-Based Invoice

Customer R-30000 had a monitor failure and needs to rent item R-30000-01 for Friday, February 1 only. They would be picking up the item at 4 PM and returning the item at 9 AM on Monday, February 4 because CRONUS is closed over the weekend. You inform the customer that the daily rate for this item is normally 25.00 but since it is an emergency and they are a good customer, you will give them the prorated monthly rate of 12.50/day.

Create the Rent Order

Follow these steps to create the rent order:

  1. Insert a new rent order for customer R-30000.
  2. On the first line of the Lines FastTab, enter the following:
    1. Type = Item
    2. No. = R-30000-01
    3. Quantity = 1
    4. Rental Rate Code = Month
    5. Rental Start Date = 02/01/19
    6. Rental End Date = 02/04/19
    7. Rental Shipment Time = 4:00 PM
  3. Notice that the Unit Price Total Rental Time is 38.89, which is calculated by the Rate Unit Price / * Total No. of Rate Units (350 * 11).

: Rent Order with prorated monthly rate pricing

Process the Shipment and Confirm no Invoice

Follow these steps to process the shipment:

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. With the Ship option selected, click OK.
  3. On the ribbon, click Post.
  4. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  5. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  6. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 02/04/19.
  7. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  8. Note that no invoices were created.

Process the Return and Create the Invoice

Follow these steps to process the shipment:

  1. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/04/19.
  2. In the Lines FastTab, enter 02/04/19 in the Rental Return Date field.
  3. In the Rental Return Time field, enter 9:00 AM.
  4. In the Return Qty. to Receive field, enter 1.
  5. On the ribbon, click Post.
  6. Select the Receive option and then click OK.
  7. Notice that the Unit Price Total Rental Time is updated to 37.50, which is calculated by the Rate Unit Price / * Total No. of Rate Units (350 * 11).

: Hourly-based rent order with updated return pricing

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  3. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  4. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 02/04/19.
  5. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  6. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  7. Open the invoice and notice that the hourly-based pricing (37.50) is used.

: Invoice with hourly-based pricing

  1. Post the invoice and then escape back to the Rent Order.
