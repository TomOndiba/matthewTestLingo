Rent Reservation Positions

In the Rent Reservation, users can enter positions to be reserved for rent (items) by showing them on the disposition calendar. Only items that have the Rent Item field set to Yes on the Item Card can be rented.

Exercise – Record a Rental Position on a Rent Reservation

On January 29, 2019, customer R-10000 requests one unit of item R-10000-01 for the period of February 1st through February 28th.

Follow these steps to record the rental position:

  1. In the navigation pane, click cc|rental > Contract Management > Rent Reservations.
  2. On the ribbon, click New.
  3. In the Sell-to Customer No. field, enter R-10000.
  4. In the Lines FastTab, select Item in the Type field.
  5. In the No. field, enter R-10000-01 and then press ENTER. Notice the following:
    • The Rental Position field changed from Sales to Rental.
    • The RENTAL location defaults to the Location Code field (if assigned to the customer).
    • The customer location (R-10000) defaults to the Customer Location Code field.
    • The Rent Line Details FactBox displays 20 available units and a rental price for the item.

: Rent Reservation with portion of line entered

  1. In the Quantity field, type 1.
  2. In the Rental Rate Code field, enter MONTH. Notice that the Rate Unit Price field is populated with a value from the rent prices setup.
  3. In the Rental Start Date field, enter 02/01/19. Notice that the Rent Line Details FactBox now displays 19 available units of item R-10000-01.
  4. In the Rental End Date field, enter 02/28/19.

: Rent Reservation with remainder of line entered

  1. On the ribbon, click Release.
