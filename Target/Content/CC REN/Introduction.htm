Introduction

cc|rental is a business management solution – built on Microsoft Dynamics® NAV – for small and mid-sized organizations which automates and streamlines business processes. Highly adaptable cc|rental enables companies to manage areas of their renting business.

The solution is not specific to any particular rentals industry sector; it is generic for all types of rental businesses.

Key Features

  • Rent Item
    • Definition of item used also for renting
    • Filtering of items only to be sold
    • Separate pricing for each Rate Code
    • Separate line discounts for each Rate Code
    • Rate Structure
    • Rental Rates for several (fixed) periods of time
    • Pricing for Customer, Customer Price Group, Currency, Item Unit of Measure and Rental Rate
    • Line Discounts for Customer, Customer Price Group, Currency, Item Unit of Measure and Rental Rate
    • Rate Components – additional positions to be invoiced with the rented item as well periodically parallel to the item rent or once at the beginning or at the end of the rental period
  • Invoice Periods
    • Selectable Invoice Periods with adjustable Due Date (Start / End of Invoicing Period) that can be different from the Rate Period
  • Rent Reservation
    • Rent Reservations can be used to reserve rental items
    • Reservations can be transferred to Rent Orders
    • Reservations can be used with the Graphical Disposition Calendar
  • Rent Quote
    • Rental Quotes can be used to have items offered for a specific period to the customer
    • Rental of item, resources, and G/L account positions
    • Quotes can be transferred to Rent Orders
  • Rent Order
    • Rental Contracts that can handle renting positions and sales positions
    • Rental of item, resources and G/L account positions
    • Rental Failure with adjustable credit memo percentage
    • Open-ended Rental Contracts
    • Default values for all Rental lines in Contract Header
    • Extension of Rental Period
    • Rate Parts and One-Time Invoicing for rented items
    • One Time invoicing (sales) of consumable supplies from the Rental Contract
    • Item Availability by Rental Period (least availability)
    • Transporting Time adjustable for each line to be considered in availability information
    • Allocation of expenses unrelated to accounting period for prepaid contracts
    • 3rd Party Rentals for out-of-stock items
    • Leasing Agreement capabilities
    • Rent Orders can be used with the Graphical Disposition Calendar
  • Service
    • Selectable requirement of Service Item (no rental of serial number-based item if Service Item does not exist)
    • Internal Service Orders
  • Machines
    • Serial number-based items with special attributes used in Purchase, Sales, Service or Rentals
    • Can be connected to Fixed Assets
  • Graphical Disposition Calendar
    • Graphical view of items that are handled with serial numbers and are created from the Machine Type Card

Access the cc|rental module from three areas on the Departments menu on the navigation pane:

  • Machines
  • cc|rental
  • cc|graphical extension (used for the Graphical Disposition Calendar)
