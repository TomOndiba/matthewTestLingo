Exercise – Create a Leasing Agreement with a Down Payment

Customer R-30000 rents item R-30000-02 (JLB VRX918S 18 in. Subwoofer) so often that they have asked the rental company if it would consider a lease agreement with an option to buy the item.

On January 27, 2019, the rental company and the customer agree that one unit of item R-30000-02 over the course of two months (February 1st – March 31st) is 75.00 per month (billed prepaid) with a 150.00 down payment. At the end of the leasing agreement, the customer can buy the item for 850.00.

Create the Rent Order and Lease Agreement

Follow these steps to create the rent order and lease agreement:

  1. Set the work date to 01/27/19.
  2. Insert a new rent order for customer R-30000.
  3. On the first line of the Lines FastTab, enter the following:
    1. Type = Item
    2. No. = R-30000-02
    3. Quantity = 1
  4. On the Lines FastTab, click Functions > Leasing Agreement.
  5. On the ribbon, click New.
  6. In the Starting Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  7. In the Contract Period field, type 2M.
  8. In the Rental Rate Code field, enter Month.
  9. In the Down Payment field, type 150.
  10. In the Leasing Rate field, type 50.
  11. Select the Prepaid check box.
  12. In the Sales Amount field, type 850.

: Leasing Agreement page

Create the Down Payment Order

Follow these steps to create the Down Payment Order:

  1. On the ribbon, click Create Down Payment Order.
  2. Click OK to close the confirmation message that “Order XXXX was created.”
  3. Notice that the Down Payment Order No. field on the Leasing Agreement page contains the number noted in the confirmation message.

: Leasing Agreement page with down payment order number

  1. On the ribbon, click Open Down Payment Order. Notice the following:
    • Date fields are set to the Work Date
    • G/L account specified in the Leasing Account field on the General Posting Setup page is used
    • Unit Price is set to the Down Payment amount on the Leasing Agreement

: Down Payment sales order

  1. Click OK to close the Sales Order page.
  2. Click OK to close the Leasing Agreement pages and return to the Rent Order. Notice that the:
    • Leasing field is set to Yes on the rent order line.
    • Information from the Leasing Agreement transferred to the rent order line

: Leasing indicated on the rent order line

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. With the Ship option selected, click OK. The following message appears:

: Error message received from attempting to ship a rent order with an open Down Payment Order

  1. Click OK to close the error message.
  2. On the Lines FastTab, click Functions > Leasing Agreement.
  3. On the ribbon, click Open Down Payment Order.
  4. On the ribbon, click Post.
  5. With the Ship and Invoice option selected, click OK.
  6. On the ribbon of the Leasing Agreement page, click Refresh. Notice that the:
    • Down Payment Order No. field is now blank
    • Posted Down Payment No. field contains the posted sales order number

: Posted Down Payment Order information on Leasing Agreement

  1. Click OK to close the Leasing Agreement page.

Ship and Invoice the Rent Order

Follow these steps to ship and invoice the Rent Order:

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. With the Ship option selected, click OK.
  3. On the ribbon, click Post.
  4. Select the Invoice option and then click OK.
  5. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  6. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 02/28/19.
  7. Click OK and accept all confirmation messages.
  8. Open the Service Invoice Rental and notice that the line is to be posted to the Leasing Prepayment Account.
  9. Post the invoice and then return to the Rent Order.
  10. Set the Work Date to 03/01/19.
  11. On the ribbon of the Rent Order, click Home > Post.
  12. Select the Invoice option and then click OK.
  13. In the Posting Date field, enter 03/01/19.
  14. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  15. Click OK and accept all confirmation messages.
  16. Open and post the Service Invoice Rental.

Sell the Leased Item

Follow these steps to sell the leased item from the Rent Order:

  1. In the Posting Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  2. On the Rent Order line, enter 03/31/19 in the Rental Return Date field.
  3. In the Return Qty. to Receive field, enter 1.
  4. Use the “Choose Columns” function to add the Sell Item from Rent field to the Lines FastTab.
  5. Select the Sell Item from Rent check box on the rental item line.

: Rent Order with updated return information and sale setting

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Functions > Sales Allocation. Notice that the:
    • Invoiced Rental Profit field is populated with the total amount that has been invoiced for the rental position.
    • Non-Charge Sales Price field is populated with the Sales Amount from the Leasing Agreement.

: Sales Allocation with information from Leasing Agreement

  1. Click OK to close the Sale from Rental page.
  2. On the ribbon of the Rent Order, click Home > Post.
  3. Select the Receive option and then click OK. Notice the following:
    • The return was processed on the original item line (and any relevant rate components).
    • A Sales line is added that contains the calculated Sales Price as the Unit Price.

: Sales line added to returned rent order

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. With the Ship option selected, click OK.
  3. On the ribbon, click Post.
  4. Select the Invoice option and then click OK.
  5. In the Posting Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  6. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  7. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  8. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  9. Open the invoice and notice that item R-30000-02 appears on the line with the calculated Sales Price as the unit price.

: Sales invoice for the leased item

  1. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  2. Escape back to the rent order.

Note

If the return was already posted, manually create a Sales Order from the Rent Order by clicking Actions > Create Sales Order on the ribbon.
