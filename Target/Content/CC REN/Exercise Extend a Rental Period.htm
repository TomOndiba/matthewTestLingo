Exercise – Extend a Rental Period

On January 31, 2019, customer R-30000 places a rent order for item R-30000-01 for the period of February 1, 2019 through March 31, 2019. This customer has a prepaid two month billing contract with your company.

On February 20th, the customer informs you that they need to extend the contract through May 31, 2019.

Create, Ship, and Invoice the Rent Order

Follow these steps to create the rent order:

  1. Set the Work Date to 01/31/19.
  2. Insert a new rent order for customer R-30000.
  3. On the first line of the Lines FastTab, enter the following:
    1. Type = Item
    2. No. = R-30000-01
    3. Quantity = 1
    4. Rental Rate Code = Month
    5. Invoice Period = Two Months
    6. Prepaid = Yes
    7. Rental Start Date = 02/01/19
    8. Rental End Date = 03/31/19
  4. On the ribbon, click Post.
  5. With the Ship option selected, click OK.
  6. On the ribbon, click Post.
  7. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  8. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  9. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  10. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  11. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  12. Open the invoice and notice that the service line is to be posted to the prepaid rental account and the range is for the two month invoicing period.

: Prepaid invoice for the two month invoicing period

  1. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  2. Escape back to the rent order.

Extend the Rental Period

Follow these steps to extend the rental period:

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Rental Extension.

: Extend Rental Period confirmation message

  1. Click Yes to extend the rental period.
  2. In the New End Date field, enter 05/31/19.
  3. In the Extension Comment field, type “Per customer request 2/20/19”.
  4. Click OK to close the Rental Extension Card page.
  5. Click the value in the Qty. Extensions field.

: Rental period extended to 5/31/2019

  1. Escape back to the rent order.

Process the Final Invoice and Return

Follow these steps to process the final invoice and complete the return:

  1. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  2. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  3. In the Posting Date field, enter 04/01/19.
  4. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 05/31/19.
  5. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  6. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  7. Open the invoice and then on the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the invoice.
  8. Escape back to the rent order.
  9. In the Posting Date field, enter 05/31/19.
  10. In the Rental Return Date field, enter 05/31/19.
  11. In the Return Qty. to Receive field, enter 1.
  12. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  13. Select the Receive option and then click OK.
  14. Notice that the Return Qty. Received field is 1.
