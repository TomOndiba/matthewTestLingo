Leasing Agreement Page

The Leasing Agreement is set up before the Rent Order ships and contains all information about duration, rate, and sales price (if needed). The values entered in the Leasing Agreement are used to populate the line values in the Rent Order.

Leasing Agreements can be archived and are stored with the archived Rent Orders.

The Leasing Agreement page is accessed from the Rent Order by selecting the proper line, and then clicking Functions > Leasing Agreement on the Lines FastTab.

Note

The initial page is non-editable if a Leasing Agreement does not exist; click New to create a new record.

: Leasing Agreement page

The fields in this page are described in the following table.

Field

Description

Agreement No.

Unique number of the Leasing Agreement.

Starting Date

Date the lease agreement begins. Transfers to the Rental Start Date field on the Rent Order line.

Contract Period

Length of the leasing agreement

End Date

Lease agreement end date calculated based on the Starting Date and Contract Date. Transfers to the Rental End Date field on the Rent Order line.

Rental Rate Code

Identifies the period to which the price relates; the actual price comes from the Leasing Rate. Transfers to the Rental Rate Code field on the Rent Order line.

Down Payment

Amount that the customer must pay to initiate the lease agreement.

Leasing Rate

Agreed upon amount of the lease per Rental Rate Code. Transfers to the Rate Unit Price field on the Rent Order line.

Invoice Period

Determines how the periods on the invoice are created. Transfers to the Invoice Period field on the Rent Order line.

Prepaid

If selected, the invoice periods are due at the first date of the period rather than the last date. Transfers to the Prepaid field on the Rent Order line.

Total Leasing Amount

Automatically calculated as (Leasing Rate * length of lease) + Down Payment.

Sales Amount

Sales price if the customer wants to purchase the leased item. This amount is automatically used in the sales line (on the Rent Order or the Sales Order) when the Sell Item from Rent check box is selected on the Rent Order line and the return is processed.

Agreement Date

System date on which the leasing agreement was created.

Down Payment Order No.

Number of the open Down Payment Order assigned to the lease agreement.

Posted Down Payment No.

Number of the posted Down Payment Order assigned to the lease agreement.
