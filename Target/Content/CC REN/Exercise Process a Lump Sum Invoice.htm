Exercise – Process a Lump Sum Invoice

Customer R-30000 has agreed to pay 3350.00 to rent item R-30000-01 starting February 1, 2019 through December 31, 2019. This item typically rents for 350.00 per month but since it is rarely rented in September and October, an agreement was reached to pay this reduced amount, in full, at the start of the rental period.

You create the rent order with Invoice Period = None, update the pricing and post the invoice for the full amount.

Set Up the Price Structure

Follow these steps to set up the price structure:

  1. Ensure that the work date is 01/26/19.
  2. In the navigation pane, click cc|rental > Contract Management > Items.
  3. Locate item R-30000-01 and then click Navigate > Prices (in the Rental group).
  4. In the Rental Prices page, enter the following:
    1. Sales Type = All Customers
    2. Unit of Measure Code = PCS
    3. Rental Rate Code = MONTH
    4. Unit Price = 350

: Rental Prices for item R-30000-01

Create the Rent Order

Follow these steps to create the rent order:

  1. Insert a new rent order for customer R-30000.
  2. On the first line of the Lines FastTab, enter the following:
    1. Type = Item
    2. No. = R-30000-01
    3. Quantity = 1
    4. Rental Rate Code = Month
    5. Invoice Period = None
    6. Prepaid = Yes
    7. Rental Start Date = 02/01/19
    8. Rental End Date = 12/31/19
    9. Invoice Multiple = Yes
  3. Notice that the Unit Price Total Rental Time is 3850.00, which is calculated by the Rate Unit Price * Total No. of Rate Units (350 * 11).

: Rent Order with Invoice Period = None and standard rate pricing

Change Pricing

Follow these steps to change the pricing on the line:

  1. In the Unit Price Total Rental Time field, enter 3350.
  2. Notice that the Total No. of Rate Units changed to 9.57, which is calculated by the Unit Price Total Rental Time / Rate Unit Price (3350 / 350).

: Updated pricing on rent order line

Process the Invoices

Follow these steps to process the invoices:

  1. On the ribbon, click Post.
  2. With the Ship option selected, click OK.
  3. On the ribbon, click Post.
  4. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  5. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  6. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 12/31/19.
  7. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  8. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  9. Open the invoice and notice that the agreement pricing (3350.00) is used.

: Invoice with lump sum agreement pricing

  1. Post the invoice and then escape back to the Rent Order.
  2. On the Lines FastTab, notice that the Quantity Invoiced field is not updated for the rental position. This field is updated when the return is posted.
