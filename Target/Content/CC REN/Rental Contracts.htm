Rental Contracts

The rental contract is the basis for renting goods and services. It contains information about the period of time and the price a period is agreed upon to be paid. cc|rental has three types of contracts:

  • Rent Reservations – Items can be chosen for reservation; the Rent Reservation documents can be found on the Disposition Calendar.
  • Rent Quotes – Estimate of the rental agreement terms for items or services; can be converted to a Rent Order.
  • Rent Orders – Rental agreement terms for items or services that is processed (shipped, invoiced and returned). Rent Order documents can be found on the Disposition Calendar.
