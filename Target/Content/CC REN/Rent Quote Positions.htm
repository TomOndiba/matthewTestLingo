Rent Quote Positions

In the Rent Quote, users can enter positions to be offered for rent (items) and also positions to be sold (items, resources, and G/L accounts) along with the rental.

Only items that have the Rent Item field set to Yes on the Item Card can be rented; items without this attribute can be sold only.

Sales positions are entered the same as a regular sales line while the rental positions have more requirements.

Exercise – Record a Sales Position on a Rent Quote

Follow these steps to fill in a sales position on the rent quote:

  1. In the navigation pane, click cc|rental > Contract Management > Rent Quotes.
  2. On the ribbon, click New.
  3. In the Sell-to Customer No. field, enter the customer number.
  4. On the Lines FastTab, leave the Rental Position set to Sales.
  5. In the Type field, enter Item, Resource or G/L Account.
  6. In the No. field, enter the relevant sales item, resource or G/L account.

Note

If a rental item is selected that is to be added as a sales line, the Rental Position will need to be reset to Sales. Any additional Rate Component lines that appeared will be removed when the Rental Position is changed.

  1. In the Location Code field, enter a location if the inventory is posted using locations.
  2. In the Quantity field, enter the number of units to be sold.
  3. Fill in other standard Microsoft Dynamics NAV fields as needed.

Exercise – Record a Rental Position on a Rent Quote

On January 31, 2019, customer R-30000 requests one unit of item R-30000-01 for the period of February 1, 2019 through February 28, 2019. The rental price is 350.00 for all customers. The customer accepts the quote and is converted to an order.

Record the Rental Position

Follow these steps to record the rental position:

  1. Set the Work Date to 01/31/19.
  2. In the navigation pane, click cc|rental > Contract Management > Rent Quotes.
  3. On the ribbon, click New.
  4. In the Sell-to Customer No. field, enter R-30000.
  5. In the Lines FastTab, in the Type field, select Item.
  6. In the No. field, enter R-30000-01. Notice the following:
    • The Rental Position field changed from Sales to Rental.
    • The RENTAL location defaults to the Location Code field (if assigned to the customer).
    • The customer location (R-30000) defaults to the Customer Location Code field.
    • The Unit Price Excl. VAT field is blank because no standard unit price has been assigned on the item card.
    • The Rent Line Details FactBox displays 35 available units and a sales price for the item.

: Rent Quote with portion of line entered

  1. In the Quantity field, type 1.
  2. In the Rental Rate Code field, enter MONTH. Notice that the Rate Unit Price field is 350.00, which is from the rent prices setup.
  3. In the Rental Start Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  4. In the Rental End Date field, enter 02/28/19.

: Rent Quote with remainder of line entered

Convert the Rent Quote to a Rent Order

Follow these steps to convert the rent quote to a rent order:

  1. On the ribbon, click Make Order.
  2. Click Yes to convert the quote to an order.
