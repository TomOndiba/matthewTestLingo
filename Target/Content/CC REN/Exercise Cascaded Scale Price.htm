Exercise – Process a Rent Order with Cascaded Scale Price

Customer R-40000 signed an agreement to rent item R-40000-01 for at least six months starting February 1, 2019. This item rents for 500 per month. For this agreement, the standard rate will be invoiced for the first four months and then 485 for the last two months. If the customer chooses to extend the lease past six months, the rate will be 450 per month for the remainder of the year.

You set up a new pricing structure for this customer and then create the Rent Order, to be prepaid every three months. Prior to shipment, the customer informs you that they will extend the rental through October so you add the extra pricing in the rent order.

Note

When the Minimum Qty. Rate Units field is used in combination with Cascading = Yes, a record with Minimum Qty. Rate Units = 0 is needed in the Rental Price table as a starting price.

Set Up the Price Structure

Follow these steps to set up the price structure:

  1. Ensure that the work date is 01/26/19.
  2. In the navigation pane, click cc|rental > Contract Management > Items.
  3. Locate item R-40000-01 and then click Navigate > Prices (in the Rental group).
  4. In the Rental Prices page, enter the following:
    1. Sales Type = Customer
    2. Sales Code = R-40000
    3. Unit of Measure Code = PCS
    4. Rental Rate Code = MONTH
    5. Unit Price = 500
  5. Go to the next line and enter the following:
    1. Sales Types = Customer
    2. Sales Code = R-40000
    3. Unit of Measure Code = PCS
    4. Rental Rate Code = MONTH
    5. Minimum Qty. Rate Units = 5
    6. Unit Price = 485
  6. On either line, select the Cascading check box.
  7. A message appears asking to update existing records. This occurs because all records that are using the same conditional fields (such as Currency Code, Rental Rate Code, Unit of Measure Code , etc.) with different Minimum Qty. Rate Units must correlate in the same pricing structure.
  8. Click Yes to update existing records.

: Rental prices for non-cascaded scale price

  1. Click OK to close the Rental Prices page.

Create the Rent Order

Follow these steps to create the rent order:

  1. Insert a new rent order for customer R-40000.
  2. On the first line of the Lines FastTab, enter the following:
    1. Type = Item
    2. No. = R-40000-01
    3. Quantity = 1
    4. Rental Rate Code = Month
    5. Prepaid = Yes
    6. Rental Start Date = 02/01/19
    7. Rental End Date = 05/31/19
  3. Notice that the standard pricing is used because only four months were included in the invoicing period and that this is a cascaded pricing structure:
    1. Rate Unit Price = 500
    2. Total No. of Rate Units = 4
    3. Cascaded Price = Yes

: Standard pricing used when number of rate units not met

View Pricing Structure and Add Extra Pricing

Follow these steps to view the cascaded pricing structure and add the extra month pricing:

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Cascaded Prices (alternatively, click the value in the Cascaded Price field).
  2. Notice that two lines appear, one for the standard pricing of 500 for four months and then the special pricing of 485 starting on the fifth month with a placeholder ending date.

: Rental Cascaded Price with pricing from Rental Prices page

  1. On the second line, change the Next Invoice Period End field to 07/31/19.
  2. Go to the next line (or click New).
  3. In the New Rent Price field, enter 450.
  4. In the Next Invoice Period End field, enter to 12/31/19.

: Rental Cascaded Price with changed and extra pricing

  1. Click OK to close the Rental Cascaded Price page.
  2. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Show Price History to view all pricing changes.

: Rental Line Price History with all cascaded pricing changes

  1. Close the Rental Line Price History page.

Process the Invoices

Follow these steps to process the invoices:

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Item Tracking Lines.
  2. With the Shipment option selected, click OK.
  3. On the ribbon, click Select Entries and then click OK.
  4. Click OK to close the Item Tracking Lines page.
  5. On the ribbon, click Post.
  6. With the Ship option selected, click OK.
  7. On the ribbon, click Post.
  8. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  9. In the Posting Date field, enter 02/01/19.
  10. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 04/30/19.
  11. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  12. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  13. Open the invoice and notice that the standard pricing (500.00) is used on all lines.

: First invoice with agreement pricing

  1. Post the invoice and then escape back to the Rent Order.
  2. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  3. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  4. In the Posting Date field, enter 05/01/19.
  5. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 07/31/19.
  6. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  7. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  8. Open the invoice and notice that the standard pricing (500.00) is used for the first line (fourth month) and the agreement pricing (485.00) is used on the remaining lines.

: Second invoice with agreement pricing

  1. Post the invoice and then escape back to the Rent Order.

Invoice Remaining Months

Follow these steps to invoice the remaining three months:

  1. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  2. Click the Invoice option and then click OK.
  3. In the Posting Date field, enter 08/01/19.
  4. In the Invoice to Date field, enter 10/31/19.
  5. Click OK to create the invoice and then accept all confirmation messages.
  6. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Invoices.
  7. Open the invoice and notice that the new agreement pricing (450.00) is used on all lines.

: Third invoice with agreement pricing
