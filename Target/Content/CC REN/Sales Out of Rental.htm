Sales Out of Rental

Occasionally, a customer may decide to purchase an item that he or she has already rented. If an agreement is reached between the rental company and the customer, cc|rental can be used to process the sales activity in either the Rent Order or in a separate Sales Order.

To sell a rented item, it must first be returned on the Rental position in the Rent Order and then can be sold from a new line within the Rent Order or from a Sales Order.

: Sale from Rental page

The fields on this page are described in the following table.

Field

Description

Use Charge

If selected, the invoiced Rental amounts can be charged to the Sales Price.

Invoiced Rental Profit

Displays the total amount that has been invoiced for a Rental position.

Invoiced Rate Part Profit

Displays the total amount that has been invoiced for all Rate Part positions for the selected item line.

Invoiced One-Time Profit

Displays the total amount that has been invoiced for all One-Time Invoicing positions for the selected item line.

Sales Price

Total Sales Price to be achieved if the Sales Price is charged with the Rental Profit; manually populated.

Chargeable Rental Profit

Displays the amount of the Rental entries to be charged; underlying entries can be selected by clicking the amount.

Chargeable Rate Part Profit

Displays the amount of the Rate Part entries to be charged; underlying entries can be selected by clicking the amount.

Chargeable One-Time Profit

Displays the amount of the One-Time Invoicing entries to be charged; underlying entries can be selected by clicking the amount.

Sales Amount (per Unit)

Displays the resulting (remaining) Sales Amount calculated for one unit of the item selected.

Sales Amount (Qty.)

Displays the resulting (remaining) Sales Amount calculated for the quantity in the Sales line.

Quantity

Number of items to sell; defaults from the rent order line.

Non-Charge Sales Price

Sales Price to be used if no rental amounts are charged; manually populated.
