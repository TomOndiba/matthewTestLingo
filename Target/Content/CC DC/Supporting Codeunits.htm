Supporting Codeunits

cc|document configurator is delivered with numerous Codeunits that are designed to make the setup of some common printouts easier and to perform some modification of data during printing (such as archiving documents, updating print counters, etc.).

The following sections provide an overview and an explanation of those Codeunits (refer to topic “Write Custom Code” on page 1 for more information about creating Codeunits).
