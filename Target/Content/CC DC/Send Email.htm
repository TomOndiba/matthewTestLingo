Send Email

Where standard Dynamics NAV functionality is available (sales documents), the “Email” or “Post and Email” actions appear. Otherwise, the “Send PDF by Email” action is available. Any of the noted actions will generate an email with the sender, recipient(s), subject line, body text and the PDF document as an attachment, as well as any attachments on the distribution entries.

: Email generated from the “Send PDF by Email” function on Purchase Order

The Send E-Mail page includes the Edit in Outlook check box, which will open the email in Outlook to allow for further functionality.

: Email opened in Outlook from the Send E-Mail page

Generated emails can be tracked in the Email Distribution Entries page, which is accessible by clicking Show Email Distribution Entries on the ribbon of any page that uses the send email functionality.

: Email Distribution Entries for email generated from a Purchase Order
