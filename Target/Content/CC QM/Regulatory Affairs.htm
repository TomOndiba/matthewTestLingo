Regulatory Affairs

cc|regulatory affairs is a module-independent verification logic that allows for granting permission to execute certain sensitive processes or actions.

The areas of where the verification logic has been applied are noted in the following table.

Area

Verification for…

Inventory

Item Journal, Item Reclassification Journal, Physical Inventory Journal, Transfer Order, Lot-/Serial No. Administration

Warehouse

Receipt, Put-away, Pick, Shipment, Movements, Warehouse Journals

Sales

Sales Order, Sales Invoice, Sales Return Order, Sales Credit Memo

Purchase

Purchase Order, Purchase Invoice, Purchase Return Order, Purchase Credit Memo

Manufacturing

Certification of Production BOM / Routing, modification of components and operations within Production Orders, Output Journal, Consumption Journal

Quality Management

Certification of Test Plan, reset Test Plan status, modification of actual values of finished Test Order

Additionally, cc|regulatory affairs offers field-level security. Verification and field-level security functionalities can be used together or separately.

The cc|regulatory affairs functionalities are accessed from the navigation pane.
