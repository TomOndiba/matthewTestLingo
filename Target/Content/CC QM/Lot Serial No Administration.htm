Lot-/Serial No. Administration

The Lot-/Serial No. Administration page documents and manages all lot numbers that have a Lot No. Information Card and serial numbers that have a Serial No. Information Card (refer to topic "Information Card" on page 1). This page is accessed from the cc|quality management menu by clicking cc|quality management > Lot-/Serial No. Administration.

A range of filter criteria is available for restricting the display to certain lots only.

: Lot-/Serial No. Administration page filtered on item Q-40005 and specific lots

Once filter criteria is entered, click Get Records on the ribbon to populate the lines.
