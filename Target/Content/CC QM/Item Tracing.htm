Item Tracing

In the Item Tracing page, it is possible to display individual lot- or serial numbers (via certain filter criteria in the header). The direction of the tracking (Origin -> Usage or Usage -> Origin) can also be defined in the Trace Method field.

: Item Tracing page

Tracking is performed on a multi-level basis across all levels from purchase to sales and not only show goods movements (purchase entries, consumption postings, sales entries and so on) but modifications on lot- or serial numbers as well (e.g. changes to lot status, changes in the expiration date and so on).

Click the Description field to open the document on which the item in the respective line is based.

In the Item Tracing page, the following lot- and serial number data modifications are shown:

Serial No.

Retest Date

Lot No.

Warranty Date

External Lot No.

Entry Date

Status

Availability Date

Quarantine Date

Expiration Date

Refer to Online Help for other standard material movements in the Item Tracing page.
