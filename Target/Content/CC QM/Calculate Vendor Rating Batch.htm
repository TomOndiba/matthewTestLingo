Calculate Vendor Rating Batch Job

The “Calculate Vendor Rating” batch job – accessed from the cc|vendor rating menu by clicking Reports and Analysis > Calculate Vendor Rating – calculates a score for the hard criteria of each receipt line.

: Calculate Vendor Rating batch job

Note

This batch job does not automatically process a re-classification of the vendor. Therefore, the Vendor Rating report must be executed, which is located on the cc|vendor rating menu by clicking Reports and Analysis > Vendor Rating.

The rating of the due date, quantity and quality criteria that have been automatically created by the batch job per purchase line are transferred to the Vendor Rating Entries page.

: Calculated vendor rating entry

In the Vendor Rating Entries page, a total score is calculated per purchase line. This score is calculated by the multiplication of the individually ascertained scores for appointed time, quantity and quality.

Example

Quality score = 60

Appointed time score = 100

Quantity score = 100

That leads to a calculation of 0.6 x 1.0 x 1.0 = 0.60, therefore the Total Score = 60.

The vendor rating only considers the shipment lines that have the Duty of Rating check box selected. Shipment lines with the Cancelled check box selected are not included in the vendor rating.

Therefore, if a receipt line is cancelled using the “Undo Receipt” function, the system creates a negative shipment line. The Cancelled check box is selected on the original line and the negative shipment line, and has no effect on the vendor rating.

Note

In the batch job, the 100 Points for missing QC Rating check box can be selected. Consequently, the full score will be assigned if there is no other information about the quality rating. This is useful if a quality rating is only to be processed for deficient shipments.
