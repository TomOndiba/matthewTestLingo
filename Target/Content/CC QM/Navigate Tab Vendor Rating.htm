Navigate Tab, Vendor Rating Actions

The Navigate tab includes the following cc|vendor rating actions:

  • Soft Criterion
  • Vendor Rating Entry
  • Rating History

Each is described in this section.

Soft Criterion

In the Vendor Rating Soft page, ratings can be copied manually from the Soft Criteria Category page and be provided with ratings. Examples for soft criteria are visitor reports, audit results, or self-assessments.

: Vendor Rating Soft page

The soft ratings entered in this page are included in the overall rating for the “Calculate Vendor Rating” batch job, accessed on the cc|vendor rating menu by clicking Reports and Analysis > Calculate Vendor Rating.

Vendor Rating Entry

The Vendor Rating Entries are created based on the calculated scores of the hard rating criteria (date, quality and quantity ratings).

: Vendor Rating Entries page

Rating History

The Rating History page displays the overall rating of the respective vendor per date. The final ratings are calculated based on the weighting of the hard against the soft rating criteria.

: Rating History page
