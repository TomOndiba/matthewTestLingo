Quality Rating for Purchase Receipt Line

While the rating of date and quantity occurs automatically by the vendor rating parameters when posting the purchase line, the quality rating of every delivery (receipt) must be processed manually. The quality rating cannot be carried out until the shipment has arrived and has been registered in the receipt.

Note

A Quality Rating can be entered on the purchase line prior to receipt; however, the Quality Rating will only affect the Quality Rating Entries (created from the “Calculate Vendor Rating” batch job) when the receipt is posted.

For this purpose, the system uses the Quality Rating Purchase page, accessed from the cc|vendor rating menu by clicking cc|vendor rating > Quality Evaluation Purchase. This page shows the Posted Receipt lines that have the Duty of Rating field set to Yes and is used to fill in the Quality Rating on the Posted Receipt.

: Quality Rating Purchase page

The Quality Rating Code Document page for the respective line can be accessed by clicking Quality Rating on the ribbon.

Click the arrow in the Code field to open the list of Quality Rating Codes. From this list, select an appropriate rating code to be transferred to the Quality Rating Code Document page.

: Quality Rating Code Document page with Quality Rating Code options

Note

The Allow Rating in Posted Rcpt. check box must be selected in the Vendor Rating Setup page to select a quality rating code.

The quality rating of the shipment can be accessed from the Posted Purchase Receipt by clicking Line > Quality Rating on the Lines FastTab.
