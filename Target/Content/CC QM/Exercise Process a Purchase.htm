Exercise – Process a Purchase Order Receipt and Calculate Vendor Ratings

In this exercise, process a Purchase Order to vendor V00010 for 1000 units of item Q-40015 and add the F2 quality rating after receipt of the shipment. Then, run the “Calculate Vendor Rating” batch job to generate the vendor scores for the receipt.

Process the Purchase Order Receipt

Follow these steps to process the purchase order receipt:

  1. In the navigation pane, click Purchasing > Order Processing > Purchase Orders.
  2. On the ribbon, click New and then press ENTER to automatically populate the No. field.
  3. In the Buy-from Vendor No. field, enter V00010.
  4. In the Lines FastTab, enter Item in the Type field.
  5. In the No. field, enter Q-40015.
  6. In the Location Code field, enter MAINWH.
  7. In the Quantity field, enter 1000.
  8. In the Promised Receipt Date field, enter 01/27/19.
  9. In the Arrival Date field, enter 01/28/19.
  10. On the ribbon, click Release and then click Post.
  11. Select the Receive option and then click OK.
  12. Click OK to close the Purchase Order.

Enter Quality Evaluations for a Purchase Receipt

Follow these steps to enter quality evaluations for a purchase receipt

  1. In the navigation pane, click cc|vendor rating > cc|vendor rating > Quality Evaluation Purchase.
  2. Select the line for the Item Q-40015 / Buy-from Vendor No. V00010 combination and then click Quality Rating on the ribbon.
  3. In the Code field, enter F2, which is the code for a 90% rating.

: Quality Rating Code Document page

  1. Click OK to close the Quality Rating Code Document page.

Calculate the Vendor Score for Receipts

Follow these steps to calculate the vendor score:

  1. On the cc|vendor rating menu, click Reports and Analysis > Calculate Vendor Rating.
  2. On the Options FastTab, select the 100 points for missing QC Rating check box.
  3. On the Purch. Rcpt. Line FastTab, enter V00010 as a filter for the Buy-from Vendor No. field.

: Calculate Vendor Rating request page

  1. Click OK to start the rating calculation for vendor V00010.

View the Vendor Rating Calculation

Follow these steps to view the vendor rating calculation:

  1. In the navigation pane, click Purchasing > Order Processing > Vendors.
  2. Locate vendor V00010 and then double-click the line to open the Vendor Card.
  3. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Vendor Rating Entry.

: Vendor Rating Entries page

  1. Review the new vendor rating scores and then close the open pages.
