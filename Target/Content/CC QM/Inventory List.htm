Inventory List

The Inventory List page displays the inventory per item (production and filling items respectively). In the Inventory List, users can filter by Location and Bin.

: Inventory List page

The list contains information about the:

  • Entire available Inventory
  • Quantity on Sales Orders, Purchase Orders, Production Orders and Component Lines

In addition, the Inventory List displays information about the usage of the item within the last:

Month

6 months

3 months

12 months

By clicking the Inventory (IO) field on a line, users can access the Inventory Overview page, which will be filtered on the respective item.
