Retain Sample Management

The Retain Sample Management functionality makes shares of a lot part of a reusable item structure. The state of the item on production/shipment is fixed by taking a certain part of it and storing it for a certain period of time. The quantity and time period are set up in the Retain Sample Instructions.

Once setup is complete, the system uses the status from the Quality Management Setup page to take the entered quantity and move it as a retained sample to the new location on Sales Order Shipment and/or Production Output.
