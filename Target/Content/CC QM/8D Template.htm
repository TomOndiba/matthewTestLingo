8D Template

The 8D Template is used as the basis for the 8D Report. Once the number series are defined and assigned in Quality Management Setup, an 8D Template can be created.

: 8D Template Card

The 8D Template Card contains eight FastTabs, where seven of the eight disciplines for problem solving/resolution are defined. In the General FastTab, enter a template identifier and description. In the remaining FastTabs, select a step and then provide a description of the step that can be used to ensure a consistent methodology.

: Expanded D2 - Describe Problem FastTab, displaying Steps D1-D8

Note

Step “D1 – Establish Team” is managed on the individual 8D reports, not in the template.

Once setup is complete, the template number can be entered in the Default 8D Template No. field in the Quality Management Setup page.

: Default 8D Template No. field in Quality Management Setup
