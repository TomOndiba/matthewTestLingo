Exercise – Generate a Test Order Automatically

In this exercise, create a purchase order for 500 units of item Q-40014 and then post the receipt. This will automatically create a test order.

Review the Test Plan Setup for Item Q-40014

Follow these steps to review the Test Plan Setup for item Q-40014:

  1. On the cc|quality management menu, click cc|quality management > Items.
  2. Locate and select item Q-40014.
  3. Click Test Plan Setup on the ribbon.

: Test Plan Setup for item Q-40014

  1. Note that the item is to be tested with test plan TP00002 on purchase receipt.
  2. Close the Test Plan Setup page.

Process the Purchase Order

Follow these steps to process the purchase order:

  1. In the navigation pane, click Purchasing > Order Processing > Purchase Orders.
  2. On the ribbon, click New and then press ENTER or TAB.
  3. In the Buy-from Vendor No. field, enter V00010.
  4. In the Lines FastTab, enter Item in the Type field.
  5. In the No. field, enter Q-40014.
  6. In the Location Code field, enter MAINWH.
  7. In the Quantity field, type 500.
  8. In the No. of Units field, type 5.
  9. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Item Tracking Lines.
  10. On the ribbon, click Actions > Assign Lot No.
  11. Click OK to close the Item Tracking Lines page.
  12. On the ribbon, click Post.
  13. Select the Receive option and then click OK.
  14. Close the Purchase Order page.
  15. In the navigation pane, click cc|quality management > Test Orders per Status > Test Orders - Created.
  16. Open the test order for item Q-40014 (refer to Fig. 2).
