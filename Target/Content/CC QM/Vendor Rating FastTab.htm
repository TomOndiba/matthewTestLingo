Vendor Rating FastTab

The fields on this FastTab are described in the following table.

Field

Description

Vendor Rating Factor Soft / Vendor Rating Factor Hard

Vendor-specific weighting of the hard towards the soft rating factors.

Classification

Contains the code that the vendor was allocated to due to its vendor rating score (refer to topic "Categorization Codes" on page 1).

Duty of Rating

If selected, all items that are shipped from this vendor must be rated regardless of the settings on the Vendor Item Catalog or Item Vendor Catalog page (refer to topic "Vendor Item Catalog/Item Vendor Catalog" on page 1).

Note

A Vendor Rating FactBox (refer to Fig. 1) is available on some purchase document cards/list views to display the Vendor Rating fields (like Hard and Soft Rating Factors, and current Vendor Rating Code) from the Vendor Card.
