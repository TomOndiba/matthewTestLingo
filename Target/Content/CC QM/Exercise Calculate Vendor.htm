Exercise – Calculate Vendor Ratings Based on Hard and Soft Parameters

In this exercise, run the “Vendor Rating” batch job to generate the vendor ratings for vendors and then review the total vendor rating calculation created when you ran the “Vendor Rating” batch job.

Calculate the Vendor Rating for a Vendor

Follow these steps to calculate the vendor rating for a vendor:

  1. In the navigation pane, click cc|vendor rating > Reports and Analysis > Vendor Rating.
  2. On the Vendor FastTab, enter V00010 as a filter for the No. field.

: Vendor Rating request page

  1. Click Preview to start the calculation of the total rating of vendor V00010.

: Vendor Rating report

  1. Review the Vendor Rating report and then close the Print Preview page.

View the Total Vendor Rating Calculation

Follow these steps to view the total vendor rating calculation:

  1. In the navigation pane, click Purchasing > Order Processing > Vendors.
  2. Locate vendor V00010 and then double-click the line to open the Vendor Card.
  3. Expand the Vendor Rating FastTab and note that the Classification field is set to B.

: Classification updated on the Vendor Card

  1. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Rating History.

: Rating History for vendor V00010

  1. Review the vendor’s scores and classification history as of the current date.
  2. Close the open pages.
