Issued Recall List

The Issued Recall List report displays all customers that were included in a callback to prepare for the callback of an item(s).

The Issued Recall List request page includes the following:

  • Print Phone No. check box to print an internal list that includes the customer’s Phone No. in the lines.
  • On the Issued Recall Header FastTab, filter on the issued callback(s) to include in the report.
