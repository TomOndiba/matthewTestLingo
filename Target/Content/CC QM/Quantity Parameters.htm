Quantity Parameters

In the Vendor Rating Param Quantity page – accessed on the cc|vendor rating menu by clicking Administration > Quantity Parameters – scores are allocated for the percentage of the quantity variance between the delivered and the ordered quantity. These scores are included in the calculation of the hard vendor rating factors for the vendor.

: Vendor Rating Param Quantity page

In the Item Category Code field, this setup can be limited to a certain item category. If no item category has been specified, the setup applies to all items. The item category is assigned on the General FastTab of the Item Card.
