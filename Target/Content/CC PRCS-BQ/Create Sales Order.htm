Create Sales Order

The Sales Order can be created after the BoQ positions have been updated. If a Sales Quote was created prior to updating the BoQ positions, it must be updated and then can be used to create the Sales Order.

In the BoQ Budget Calculation page, click Actions > Sales Quotes > Update. The update dialog opens.

: Preparing to update the Sales Quote

In the dialog, click OK to update all lines. A message appears asking to modify the Sales Line. Click Yes.

Select a line with Sales Quote = Yes and then click Navigate > Sales Quote to open the updated sales quote. Click Make Order; a message appears to convert the quote to an order.

: Preparing to convert the Sales Quote to an Order

Click Yes to convert the quote to an order. A message appears asking to change the job status to Order.

: Message to change job status

Click Yes to change the job status. A message appears stating that the quote has been converted to an order and asks to open the order.

: Order creation confirmation message

Click Yes to open the order or No to finish the process.
