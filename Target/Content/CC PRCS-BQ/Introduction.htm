Introduction

Construction Works Processing is an add-on to cc|project which provides process support to Construction Works Industries through the management of Construction Projects from the sales process to the invoicing process.

The BoQ Manager provides the ability to transfer GAEB files electronically between companies for the purpose of bidding on and awarding construction projects based on a standard process. GAEB – or Gemeinsamer Ausschuss fuer Elektronik im Bauwesen, which can be translated as “Joint Committee on Electronics in Construction” – is a German organization that defines the standards in electronic data exchange for construction works industries (refer to http://www.gaeb.de/en/ for more information).

In cc|project, Construction Works Processing supports the import/export of the GAEB 90, GAEB 2000 and GAEB XML files to/from Microsoft Dynamics NAV. The GAEB files are the basis of the Calculation specifications.

Construction Works Processing uses the GAEB Konverter from T&T Datentechnik GmbH to convert the above formats into a special XML format (TML) that can be processed by Microsoft Dynamics NAV.
