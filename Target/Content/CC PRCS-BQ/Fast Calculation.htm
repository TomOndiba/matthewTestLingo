Fast Calculation

During the bidding process, it is faster to provide general costs for wages, materials, etc. than to enter estimates with specific resources and items. To assist with this, the Fast Calculation includes fields for each Cost Type (Wages, Material, External Services, Equipment and Other Costs). When values are entered into these fields, new lines are created automatically in the BoQ Budget Calculation as general components with the entered costs. The component number is taken from the Jobs Setup where General Components can be assigned for the Fast Calculation functionality.

Therefore, with the Fast Calculation, additional Job Lines do not need to be created manually in the BoQ Budget Calculation for any anticipated costs when responding to a quote request.

Fast Calculation Page

On the ribbon of the BoQ Position List page, click Fast Calculation.

: Fast Calculation page showing Wages, Materials and Ext. Service fields

Many of the fields on the Fast Calculation page are the same as fields in the BoQ Position List or BoQ Budget Calculation pages. Therefore, only the fields that are specific to the Fast Calculation functionality are described in the following table.

Field

Description

Wages Quantity

Indicates the quantity for wages, which is needed per one unit of the BoQ Position.

Wages Unit Cost (LCY)

Indicates the Direct Unit Cost [LCY] for wages, which is calculated per one unit of the BoQ Position. This field is defaulted by the Direct Unit Cost field of the general component.

Wages Charge %

Indicates the Charge % for wages, which is defaulted by the job charge based on the calculation group of the general component.

Wages Unit Price (LCY)

Indicates the Unit Price [LCY] for wages, which is calculated per one unit of the BoQ Position.

Material Direct Unit Cost (LCY)

Indicates the Direct Unit Cost [LCY] for material, which is calculated per one unit of the BoQ Position.

Material Charge %

Indicates the Charge % for material, which is defaulted by the job charge based on the calculation group of the general component.

Material Unit Price (LCY)

Indicates the Unit Price [LCY] for material, which is calculated per one unit of the BoQ Position.

Ext. Service Direct Unit Cost (LCY)

Indicates the Direct Unit Cost [LCY] for external services, which is calculated per one unit of the BoQ Position.

Ext. Service Charge %

Indicates the Charge % for external services, which is defaulted by the job charge based on the calculation group of the general component.

Ext. Service Unit Price (LCY)

Indicates the Unit Price [LCY] for external services, which is calculated per one unit of the BoQ Position.

Equipment Unit Cost (LCY)

Indicates the Unit Cost [LCY] for equipment, which is calculated per one unit of the BoQ Position.

Ext. Service Cost %

Indicates the Cost % for external services, which is defaulted by the job charge based on the calculation group of the general component.

Material Cost %

Indicates the Cost % for material, which is defaulted by the job charge based on the calculation group of the general component.

Other Unit Cost (LCY)

Indicates the Unit Cost [LCY] for other costs, which is calculated per one unit of the BoQ Position.

Other 2 Unit Cost (LCY)

Indicates the Unit Cost [LCY] for other 2 costs, which is calculated per one unit of the BoQ Position. This field is defaulted by the Direct Unit Cost field of the general component.

The actions on the ribbon are the same as on the BoQ Budget Calculation.

Data Entry in Fast Calculation

In the first row with BoQ Position Type = Normal Position, values of 60 and .50 are entered for Wage Quantity and Wage Unit Cost (LCY), respectively. The Wage Charge % field is populated based on the Extra Charge % entered in the Job Charges page for Calculation Group = WAGES. The Wages Unit Price (LCY) field is calculated based on these values.

: Wage values entered in Fast Calculation

Next, material values are entered as 100 and 30 in the Material Direct Unit Cost (LCY) and Material Charge % fields, respectively. The Material Unit Price (LCY) field is calculated based on these values.

: Material values entered in Fast Calculation

Click Navigate > Position Components to view the components that have been created by entering data into the wage and material fields.

: Components added to the position for Wages and Materials

Close the Position Components page.

In the next row, values are entered again for wages and materials.

: Wage and Material values entered in Fast Calculation

To review the changes in the BoQ Position List, click OK to close the Fast Calculation page. Notice the following on the calculated lines:

  • BoQ Positions with Unit Price (LCY) <>0 are no longer formatted in red
  • Calculation Status is set to Progress
  • Pricing transferred to the Unit Price (LCY) and Total Price Budget (LCY) fields

Additionally, the Total Price Budget (LCY) field on rollup positions 3./3. 1. contain the total of both sub-positions.

: BoQ Position List with calculated values from Fast Calculation

On the ribbon, click BoQ Budget Calculation to see how values entered in the Fast Calculation have been automatically entered in that page.

: Positions added to the BoQ Budget Calculation from Fast Calculation

Caution

If calculating begins in the Fast Calculation page, further calculation processing can continue in the BoQ Budget Calculation page, but not vice versa. Therefore, once calculations start in the BoQ Budget Calculation, the Fast Calculation cannot be used.

In the BoQ Position List page, select both calculated positions, and then click Change Calculation Status on the ribbon. The Calculation Status dialog appears.

: Change Calculation Status function run on BoQ Position List

The Calculation Status dialog indicates that the selected positions are Calculated; click OK to change the status on the rows to Calculated.

: Positions changed to Calculation Status = Calculated

The “Change Calculation Status” function can be used to change a status from Calculated back to In progress or [Empty].

Close the BoQ Position List page.
