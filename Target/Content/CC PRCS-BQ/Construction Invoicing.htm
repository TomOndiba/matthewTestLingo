Construction Invoicing

The invoicing process is much more complex than in base cc|project. In Construction Works Processing, there are:

  • Three different ways to invoice:
    • Invoice planning by Sales Code (Sales Code with Sales Channel = Performance)
    • Sales Order
    • Sales Part Invoice by Sales Order (New function “Create Part Invoice”)
  • Two different invoice bases:
    • Sales Line
    • Measurements (Measurement Summaries will be created)
  • Two different invoice models:
    • Performance per period (standard Microsoft Dynamics NAV logic)
    • Cumulative Invoicing (growing invoices, including already invoiced positions)

This section describes the different invoicing options and additional requirements such as share in costs, security or warranty deposits.

If measurements are not used for Construction Works Processing, the standard cc|project methods of Invoicing Planning or Sales Order processing are used for invoicing.
