Register Measurement Recording

Once all changes have been entered in the Measurement page, it can be registered. Registering the measurement recording is like releasing a document: it makes it available for other processes.

To register the measurement, click Register. A message asking to register the measurement document.

: Preparing to register the Measurement

Click Yes to register the measurement document. A confirmation message appears.

: Confirmation message of registered measurement document

Click OK to close the confirmation message. The Measurement Recording Card closes automatically.
