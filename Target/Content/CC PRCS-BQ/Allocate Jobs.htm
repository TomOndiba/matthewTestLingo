Allocate Jobs

When a PaC Job is created from a BoQ Job, all BoQ positions are transferred to the PaC Job. The number of assigned positions is visible in the Allocated Jobs page, accessed by clicking Navigate > Allocated Jobs.

: Allocated Jobs

Since it is possible to have BoQ positions split for processing between multiple PaC Jobs, the No. of assigned Positions field indicates how many have been assigned to the current PaC Job, and the complete number of positions in the BoQ Job are displayed in the Total No. of Positions field.

To open the BoQ Position List to view the assigned positions for the job, click the value in the No. of assigned Positions field.

: BoQ Position List opened from the Allocated Jobs page, highlighting the Assigned PaC Job No. field

If the Calculation Group Totals were created during the PaC Job creation process, the amounts allocated to the PaC Job are visible in the BoQ Budget Calculation.

: Values allocated to the PaC Job in the BoQ Budget Calculation

The allocation of BoQ positions to PaC Jobs is determined by the Assigned PaC Job No. field in the BoQ Budget Calculation. To allocate to a different PaC Job, enter the relevant number in the field. To remove the allocation of a BoQ position to a PaC Job, clear the field.

: Assigned PaC Job No. cleared in BoQ Budget Calculation

The Allocated Jobs page shows the updated position assignments.

: Different assigned and total positions for allocated job
