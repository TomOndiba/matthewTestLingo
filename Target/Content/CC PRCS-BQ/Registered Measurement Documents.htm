Registered Measurement Documents

Registered Measurements are accessible from the Role Center, Home; cc|project menu > Construction > History; and directly from the related Sales Order by clicking Navigate > Registered Measurement Documents.

: Registered Measurement Card opened from Sales Order

The Amount (LCY) field in the header is updated with the total amount from the registered lines.

If the changed quantities have been confirmed by the customer, click Autofill Confirmed Quantity to update the Confirmed Quantity field in the lines with measurement Quantity. Alternatively, the quantities can be entered on lines individually.

: Measurement quantities are confirmed

All confirmed quantities can be deleted using the “Delete Confirmed Quantity” function

To complete the confirmation process, click Confirm. A message appears asking to confirm the quantities in the measurement document.

: Preparing to confirm the registered measurement lines

Click Yes to confirm the quantities. A confirmation message appears.

: Confirmation message

Click OK to close the confirmation message. The message disappears and the Confirmed at field is populated with the system date.

: Confirmed at date updated with system date

Reset / Undo Registered Measurement Lines

On the Lines FastTab, the Functions menu has two items:

  • Reset Confirmation – clears the Confirmed Quantity and Confirmed at fields on the selected measurement lines.
  • Undo Registered Measurement Line – Creates a negative quantity line for the selected measurement lines from the Measurement Document.

Undo Measurement Document

The entire Measurement Document can be undone using the “Undo Measurement Document” function on the Actions tab.
