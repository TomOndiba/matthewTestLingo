Invoice by Part Invoice

When invoicing by part invoices, the Cumulative Invoicing model is used. With this model, partial invoices are created for measurements as they occur during the construction process. These invoices contain the measurement quantity, not the sales order quantity. This is done so that the original Sales Order quantities are not modified. Partial invoices are sent to the customer with either adjustments for cumulative invoices and payment, or just payments.

For each new partial invoice, the previous partial invoices are moved back into the new partial invoice for internal accounting adjustments.

Once the Construction process is complete, measurement quantities are taken over to the order quantities, previous partial invoice is moved back into the sales order and a single line is added to the end of the sales order with a negative quantity and amount to offset the measurement changes.

Prerequisites

Before creating the invoice, the following settings must be done:

  • Job Card, Invoice Model = Cumulative Invoicing
  • BoQ Budget Calculation = Sales Code must have Posting by = Sales Order
  • Registered Measurement has been created and confirmed
  • Report Selections Sales = Invoice set to report 5292686

Open the Sales Order

When it is time to post an order with measurements, open the Job Card and click the value in the Orders field of the Project Information FactBox.

: Opening the Sales Order from the Job Card

In the Sales Orders list page, click Edit. The Sales Order opens with Job positions, registered measurement values (as confirmed in section “Registered Measurement Documents” on page 1) and proper invoice settings in the Construction Invoicing FastTab.

: Sales Order with positions and registered measurements

Create Part Invoice

The partial invoice is created based on registered measurements in the Sales Order lines. Click Navigate > Create Part Invoice. A message appears asking to create the partial invoice.

: Message to create a partial invoice

Click Yes to create the partial invoice, No to stop the creation process. The Sales Invoice opens.

: Partial Sales Invoice for registered measurements

Notice that the Base Calculation FactBox includes original calculation information about the selected position.

Also note that the Measurement Summary Code field is populated in the Construction Invoicing FastTab. Click Navigate > Measurement Summary to view the summary document that will be used for printing.

: Measurement Summary assigned to Sales Order

Click OK to close the Measurement Summary page.

Add Construction Charges to Invoice

Depending on customer agreements, special calculations can be entered now for the invoice. Since the template was assigned to the job, the Invoicing document is copied automatically when the “CS Invoice Calculation” function is run. On the Navigate tab, click CS Invoice Calculation. The CS Invoicings page opens.

: Running the CS Invoice Calculation function

Click Edit to open the Invoicing page.

: Invoicing page with Charge percentages assigned in Job Template

Change the Status to Released and then click Calculate.

: Invoicing released and ready for calculation

The Value and Cum. Value fields are calculated.

: Values updated with Calculate function

To transfer these values to the Sales Invoice, click Transfer Values. The Calculation Value Transfer page opens.

: Calculation Value Transfer page with calculated values

Charges (in the top of the page) can be transferred below any selected line in the Transfer to Line(s) FastTab but are typically added to the end of the invoice. To transfer both charges to the end of the invoice, select both charges and then select the last line in the Transfer to Line(s) FastTab.

: Both charge lines to be transferred below the last invoice line

Click Transfer Values to complete the transfer process.

: Charges have been transferred below the last invoice line

Click OK to close the Calculation Value Transfer and Invoicing pages, click Close to close the CS Invoicings page.

In the Sales Invoice, notice that the charges have been added below the last sales line.

: Charges transferred to Sales Invoice

Update Sales Order

Prior to posting the sales invoice, ensure that the Posting Date is correct in the General FastTab.

Post Sales Invoice and Print Document

Click Home > Post to post the sales invoice. After posting, click Yes to open the Posted Sales Invoice. The Construction Invoicing FastTab displays related construction information.

:Posted Sales Invoice

Click Actions > Print to open the Sales - Invoice dialog (for report 5292686). The BoQ Print Options FastTab includes special printing options. Select the Print Measurement Lines check box.

: Sales - Invoice print dialog

Click Print to generate a document or Preview to view the document. The Sales Invoice document contains measurement information as well as Construction Charges.

: Sales Invoice with Measurements and Construction Charges

Escape back to the Sales Order.

Add New Measurements

As previously stated, invoices are created through the construction process for all measurements until all positions have been invoiced. This section shows how to add measurements.

In the Sales Order, click Home > Measurement Document. A message appears asking to insert the complete BoQ in the measurement document.

: Creating a new Measurement Document for the sales order

Click Yes to insert the complete BoQ. A new Measurement Recording Card opens.

: New Measurement Recording Card for job

The process of updating the Measurement Recording Card is the same as described in topic .

Populate the Measurement Date, Measurement Page No. and Measurement Location fields, being sure to update the lines as well.

: Measurement fields updated in header and lines

In the Lines FastTab, enter the measured quantities in the Quantity field. If any new measurements were taken on registered quantities, the Cum. Quantity field will show the cumulative total. For example, three lines have registered quantities (4, 4, and 7, respectively). New totals are entered for all three lines (5, 4, and 6, respectively) and the Cum. Quantity field shows the cumulative total of the new Quantity and Cum. Registered Quantity (9, 8, and 13, respectively).

: New measurements added for registered positions and new positions

Click Register and then click Yes to register the document. The Measurement Recording Card closes.

Create Second Invoice

Now that new measurements have been added to the sales order, the next invoice can be created using the same process described previously.

: Sales order with new and previously invoiced lines

Notice that the registered measurement quantities appear in the lines, taking special notice that the measurement quantity (9) for position 3. 1. 50. exceeds the order quantity (8) by 1.

Create Part Invoice

Now that new measurements have been added, a new partial invoice can be created. Click Navigate > Create Part Invoice. A message appears asking to create the partial invoice.

Click Yes to create the partial invoice, No to stop the creation process. The Sales Invoice opens.

: Partial Sales Invoice for registered measurements

The Measurement Summary Code field is populated in the Construction Invoicing FastTab. Click Navigate > Measurement Summary to view the summary document that will be used for printing.

: Measurement Summary assigned to Sales Invoice

Click OK to close the Measurement Summary page.

Move Part Invoices

Partial Invoices that have been posted need to be moved from the invoice back to the Sales Order so that previously posted entries are not doubled during posting.

On the Navigate tab of the Sales Invoice, click Move Part Invoices. The Select Posted Sales Invoices page opens.

: Select Posted Sales Invoices opened from Move Part Invoices function

With the line selected for the previously posted partial sales invoice, click OK. A negative entry is added to the end of the sales invoice.

: Negative entry added to Sales Invoice to adjust posted partial invoice

: Both charge lines to be transferred below the last invoice line

: Charges have been transferred below the last invoice line

Add New Measurements

In the Sales Order, click Home > Measurement Document. A message appears asking to insert the complete BoQ in the measurement document.

Click Yes to insert the complete BoQ. A new Measurement Recording Card opens.

The process of updating the Measurement Recording Card is the same as described in topic .

Populate the Measurement Date, Measurement Page No. and Measurement Location fields, being sure to update the lines as well.

: Measurement fields updated in header and lines

In the Lines FastTab, enter the measured quantities in the Quantity field. Since this is the last invoice, all open positions should have registered measurements or new measurements. For example, the first four positions have new quantities, the next three positions have registered quantities, and the last four have new and registered quantities. Notice that a new measurement was not entered for position 3. 1. 70., which means the final measurement quantity is less than the order quantity. This will be handled in the Take over Measurement process in the Sales Order.

: New measurements added for registered positions and new positions

Click Register and then click Yes to register the document. The Measurement Recording Card closes.

Create Final Invoice

Now that all measurements have been added to the sales order, the final invoice can be created using various process described previously.

: Final sales order with new and previously invoiced lines

Notice that the registered measurement quantities appear in the lines, taking special notice that the measurement quantity (13) for position 3. 1. 70. is less than the order quantity (14) by 1.

Take Over Measurements

In the Sales Order, click Reopen (if Released) and then click Actions > Take over Measurements. A message appears asking to update the order quantity from the measurement quantity.

Click Yes to continue the process. A message appears asking to keep or update the order quantity.

: Keep or update order quantity message

Select the Update order quantity option and then click OK. In all lines, The Quantity and Qty. to Ship fields are updated to match the Measurement Quantity.

: Quantity and Qty. to Ship updated with measurement quantities

The Measurement Summary Code field is now populated in the Construction Invoicing FastTab. Click Navigate > Measurement Summary to view the summary document that will be used for printing.

: Measurement Summary assigned to Sales Order with cumulative quantities

Print Posted Sales Invoice without Past Invoices

If none of the partial invoices have been paid by the customer, the Print Invoice/Payment History field can be set to Payments in the Sales - Invoice dialog.

: Sales - Invoice print dialog with Payments setting

Using this setting will omit previous invoices and show any posted payments.
