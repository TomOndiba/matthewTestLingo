Job Types Setup

In addition to the standard cc|project fields in the Job Types Setup, the Default Structure Type field indicates the Structure Type that will be defaulted to the Job Card each time a Job is created with this Job Type.

: Job Types Setup with Default Structure Type

The options in this field are:

  • Bill of Quantities – used for importing/exporting GAEB files, job calculations and invoicing
  • Process and Controlling – used for planning and job costing.
  • BoQ and PaC – used for simple, small specifications that can be processed in one job.
