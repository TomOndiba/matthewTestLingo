cc|alloy management

The add-on solution cc|alloy management is specifically designed for companies whose feedstocks are subject to strong price fluctuations through exchange traded raw material. These fluctuations are passed by alloy surcharges from suppliers to customers. cc|alloy management provides the business support to manage these alloy surcharges.

Key Features

  • Management and billing of alloy surcharges
  • Assignment of alloy weights to items
  • Management of different quotations
  • Individual billing options depending on customer/vendor and item
