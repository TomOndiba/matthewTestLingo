Invoice by Sales Order

When invoicing by sales order, invoices can be created using the Performance per Period or Cumulative Invoicing models. This section addresses the Performance per Period model.

With this model, measurements are taken over to the sales orders first by updating the Qty. to Ship with the differing quantities and then invoices are posted. At the end of the project, a final invoice is created with all measurement quantities replacing sales order quantities.

Note

In this section, Construction Charges are not added to the invoices but can be added in the Sales Orders before posting using the same process as described in section .

Prerequisites

Before creating the invoice, the following settings must be done:

  • Job Card, Invoice Model = Performance per Period
  • BoQ Budget Calculation = Sales Code must have Posting by = Sales Order
  • Sales Order, Invoice Base = Measurement
  • Registered Measurement has been created and confirmed
  • Report Selections Sales = Invoice set to report 5292686

Post Sales Orders with Measurements

When it is time to post an order with measurements, open the Job Card and click the value in the Orders field of the Project Information FactBox.

: Opening the Sales Order from the Job Card

In the Sales Orders list page, click Edit. The Sales Order opens with Job positions, registered measurement values (as confirmed in section “Registered Measurement Documents” on page 1) and proper invoice settings in the Construction Invoicing FastTab.

: Sales Order with positions and registered measurements

In the Construction Invoicing FastTab, fill in the Period of performance from and Period of performance to fields with the range of the measurement invoicing.

: Invoicing range entered in Construction Invoicing FastTab

Take Over Measurements

The “Take over Measurements” function compares the measurement quantities with the order quantities. There are two options for taking over the measurements:

  • Keep order quantities – Measurement quantities are transferred to the Qty. to Ship field and the order quantity remains unchanged, unless the measurement quantity exceeds the order quantity. In that case, the order quantity is updated to match the measurement quantity. This option is used during the iteration posting so that the measurement quantities are posted.
  • Update order quantities – Order quantities are updated to the measurement quantities. This option is used for the final invoice posting.

In the Sales Order, click Actions > Take over Measurements. A message appears asking to update the order quantity from the measurement quantity.

: Running the Take over Measurements function

Click Yes to continue the process or No to cancel the process. A message appears asking to keep or update the order quantity.

: Keep or update order quantity message

With the Keep order quantity option selected, click OK. The Qty. to Ship field is cleared on all lines except those with measurement values, and contains the Measurement Quantity, not the order quantity.

: Qty. to Ship updated with measurement quantities; order quantities unchanged

Also note that the Measurement Summary Code field is now populated in the Construction Invoicing FastTab. Click Navigate > Measurement Summary to view the summary document that will be used for printing.

: Measurement Summary assigned to Sales Order

Click OK to close the Measurement Summary page.

Post Sales Order and Print Document

Click Home > Post to ship and invoice the sales order. Notice that the Qty. to Ship field is updated with all remaining quantities and the Qty. Shipped shows the shipped values. Also notice that the Measurement Summary Code field is cleared in the Construction Invoicing FastTab since the measurement has been processed.

: Sales Order after posting

Click Navigate > Invoices > View to open the Posted Sales Invoice. The Construction Invoicing FastTab displays related construction information.

: Posted Sales Invoice

Click Actions > Print to open the Sales - Invoice dialog (for report 5292686). The BoQ Print Options FastTab includes special printing options. Select the Print Measurement Lines check box.

: Sales - Invoice dialog, preparing to preview the Sales Invoice with measurement lines

Click Print to generate a document or Preview to view the document. The Sales Invoice document contains measurement information.

: Sales Invoice with Measurements

Escape back to the Sales Order.

Add New Measurements

As previously stated, invoices are created through the construction process for all measurements until all positions have been invoiced. This section shows how to add measurements.

In the Sales Order, click Home > Measurement Document. A message appears asking to insert the complete BoQ in the measurement document.

: Creating a new Measurement Document for the sales order

Click Yes to insert the complete BoQ. A new Measurement Recording Card opens.

: New Measurement Recording Card for job

The process of updating the Measurement Recording Card is the same as described in topic .

Populate the Measurement Date, Measurement Page No. and Measurement Location fields, being sure to update the lines as well.

: Measurement fields updated in header and lines

In the Lines FastTab, enter the measured quantities in the Quantity field. If any new measurements were taken on registered quantities, the Cum. Quantity field will show the cumulative total. For example, three lines have registered quantities (4, 4, and 7, respectively). New totals are entered for all three lines (5, 4, and 6, respectively) and the Cum. Quantity field shows the cumulative total of the new Quantity and Cum. Registered Quantity (9, 8, and 13, respectively).

: New measurements added for registered positions and new positions

Click Register and then click Yes to register the document. The Measurement Recording Card closes.

Create Second Invoice

Now that new measurements have been added to the sales order, the next invoice can be created using the same process described previously.

: Sales order with new and previously invoiced lines

Notice that the registered measurement quantities appear in the lines, taking special notice that the measurement quantity (9) for position 3. 1. 50. exceeds the order quantity (8) by 1.

Take Over Measurements

In the Sales Order, click Reopen and then click Actions > Take over Measurements. A message appears asking to update the order quantity from the measurement quantity.

Click Yes to continue the process. A message appears asking to keep or update the order quantity.

: Keep or update order quantity message (also highlighting that the Sales Order has been reopened)

With the Keep order quantity option selected, click OK. The Qty. to Ship field is cleared on all lines except those with measurement values, and contains the Measurement Quantity. Position 3. 1. 50. now contains the measurement quantity as the order quantity because the measurement quantity exceeded the order quantity.

: Qty. to Ship updated with measurement quantities (less shipped); order quantity changed on position 3. 1. 50.

Also note that the Measurement Summary Code field is now populated in the Construction Invoicing FastTab. Click Navigate > Measurement Summary to view the summary document that will be used for printing.

: Measurement Summary assigned to Sales Order

Click OK to close the Measurement Summary page.

Update Sales Order

Prior to posting the sales order, in the General FastTab, update the Posting Date field.

In the Construction Invoicing FastTab, update the Period of performance from and Period of performance to fields.

: Posting Date and Period of performance updated in sales order

Post Sales Order and Print Document

Click Home > Post to ship and invoice the sales order. The Qty. to Ship field is updated with all remaining quantities, the Qty. Shipped shows the shipped values, and the Measurement Summary Code field is cleared in the Construction Invoicing FastTab since the measurement has been processed.

: Sales Order after posting

Click Navigate > Invoices > View to open the Posted Sales Invoice. The Construction Invoicing FastTab displays related construction information.

Click Actions > Print to open the Sales - Invoice dialog (for report 5292686). A new Saved Settings FastTab appears indicating that the last used options and filters have been set.

: Sales - Invoice print dialog with new Saved Settings FastTab

Click Print to generate a document or Preview to view the document. The Sales Invoice document contains measurement information.

: Sales Invoice with Measurements for only the second invoice (not cumulative)

Escape back to the Sales Order.

Add New Measurements

In the Sales Order, click Home > Measurement Document. A message appears asking to insert the complete BoQ in the measurement document.

Click Yes to insert the complete BoQ. A new Measurement Recording Card opens.

The process of updating the Measurement Recording Card is the same as described in topic .

Populate the Measurement Date, Measurement Page No. and Measurement Location fields, being sure to update the lines as well.

: Measurement fields updated in header and lines

In the Lines FastTab, enter the measured quantities in the Quantity field. Since this is the last invoice, all open positions should have registered measurements or new measurements. For example, the first four positions have new quantities, the next three positions have registered quantities, and the last four have new and registered quantities. Notice that a new measurement was not entered for position 3. 1. 70., which means the final measurement quantity is less than the order quantity. This will be handled in the “Take over Measurement” process in the Sales Order.

: New measurements added for registered positions and new positions

Click Register and then click Yes to register the document. The Measurement Recording Card closes.

Create Final Invoice

Now that all measurements have been added to the sales order, the final invoice can be created using the same process described previously.

: Final sales order with new and previously invoiced lines

Notice that the registered measurement quantities appear in the lines, taking special notice that the measurement quantity (13) for position 3. 1. 70. is less than the order quantity (14) by 1.

Take Over Measurements

In the Sales Order, click Reopen and then click Actions > Take over Measurements. A message appears asking to update the order quantity from the measurement quantity.

Click Yes to continue the process. A message appears asking to keep or update the order quantity.

: Keep or update order quantity message (also highlighting that the Sales Order has been reopened)

Select the Update order quantity option and then click OK. The Qty. to Ship field is cleared on all lines except those with measurement values and contains the Measurement Quantity except for position 3. 1. 70., which is empty because the order quantity was changed to match the completely shipped measurement quantity.

: Qty. to Ship updated with measurement quantities (less shipped)

The Measurement Summary Code field is now populated in the Construction Invoicing FastTab. Click Navigate > Measurement Summary to view the summary document that will be used for printing.

: Measurement Summary assigned to Sales Order

Click OK to close the Measurement Summary page.

Update Sales Order

Prior to posting the sales order, in the General FastTab, update the Posting Date field.

In the Construction Invoicing FastTab, update the Period of performance from and Period of performance to fields.

: Posting Date and Period of performance updated in sales order

Post Sales Order and Print Document

Click Home > Post to ship and invoice the sales order. After posting, click Yes to open the Posted Sales Invoice. The Construction Invoicing FastTab displays related construction information.

Click Actions > Print to open the Sales - Invoice dialog (for report 5292686). The Saved Settings FastTab appears indicating that the last used options and filters have been set.

: Sales - Invoice print dialog with Saved Settings FastTab

Click Print to generate a document or Preview to view the document. The Sales Invoice document contains measurement information.

: Sales Invoice with Measurements for only the second invoice (not cumulative)

The Sales Order was deleted since it has been completely processed.
