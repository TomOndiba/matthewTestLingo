Invoice by Invoice Planning

When invoicing by Invoice Planning, measurements must be entered for all positions, even if the quantities are the same because invoices are only created for positions with measurements. This makes it possible to invoice all necessary sales positions using the Invoice Planning. At the end of the Invoice Planning process, all sales positions will be completely invoiced.

The CS Invoicing Template can be used during this process to include any necessary cost shares or deductions in the invoices.

Prerequisites

Before creating the invoice, the following settings must be done:

  • Job Card, Invoice Model = Cumulative Invoicing
  • BoQ Budget Calculation = Sales Code must have Sales Channel = Performance
  • Registered Measurement has been created and confirmed
  • Report Selections Sales = Invoice set to report 5292686

Create Sales Invoice with Invoice Planning

When it is time to create an invoice, open the Job Budget Sales page from the Job Card. On the Actions tab, click Job Invoicing > Planning. In the Job Invoicing Planning dialog, click OK.

: Job Budget Sales, preparing to run the Job Invoicing Planning process

Once completed, click Job Invoicing > Release to create the sales invoice. In the Job Invoicing Release dialog, enter the posting date for the invoice and then click OK.

: Job Budget Sales, preparing to run the Job Invoicing Release process

Notice that the three lines with registered measurement values (as confirmed in section ) have been transferred to the Sales Invoice.

: Job Budget Sale, preparing to open the Sales Invoice

Click Yes in the Sales Invoice field of the Job Budget Sales line to open the Sales Invoice. Notice the same positions exist in the Sales Invoice, as well as the Construction Invoicing FastTab is updated with the Measurement Summary Code, Invoice Model from Job Card and Invoice Base from the release process.

: Sales Invoice created from Job Sales Budget

In the Construction Invoicing FastTab, fill in the Period of performance from and Period of performance to fields with the range of the measurement invoicing.

: Invoicing range entered in Construction Invoicing FastTab

Click Navigate > Measurement Summary to view the summary document that was automatically created and will be used for printing.

: Measurement Summary assigned to Sales Invoice

Click OK to close the Measurement Summary page.

Add Construction Charges to Invoice

Depending on customer agreements, special calculations can be entered now for the invoice. Since the template was assigned to the job, the Invoicing document is copied automatically when the “CS Invoice Calculation” function is run. On the Navigate tab, click CS Invoice Calculation. The CS Invoicings page opens.

: Running the CS Invoice Calculation function

Click Edit to open the Invoicing page.

: Invoicing page with Charge percentages assigned in Job Template

Change the Status to Released and then click Calculate.

: Invoicing released and ready for calculation

The Value and Cum. Value fields are calculated.

: Values updated with Calculate function

To transfer these values to the Sales Invoice, click Transfer Values. The Calculation Value Transfer page opens.

: Calculation Value Transfer page with calculated values

Charges (in the top of the page) can be transferred below any selected line in the Transfer to Line(s) FastTab but are typically added to the end of the invoice. To transfer both charges to the end of the invoice, select both charges and then select the last line in the Transfer to Line(s) FastTab.

: Both charge lines to be transferred below the last invoice line

Click Transfer Values to complete the transfer process.

: Charges have been transferred below the last invoice line

Click OK to close the Calculation Value Transfer and Invoicing pages, click Close to close the CS Invoicings page.

In the Sales Invoice, notice that the charges have been added below the last sales line.

: Charges transferred to Sales Invoice

Post Sales Invoice and Print Document

Click Home > Post to post the sales invoice. After posting, click Yes to open the Posted Sales Invoice. The Construction Invoicing FastTab displays related construction information.

: Posted Sales Invoice

Click Actions > Print to open the Sales - Invoice dialog (for report 5292686).

: Sales - Invoice print dialog

The BoQ Print Options FastTab includes special printing options. Select the Print Measurement Lines check box.

: Sales - Invoice dialog, preparing to preview the Sales Invoice with measurement lines

Click Print to generate a document or Preview to view the document. The Sales Invoice document contains measurement information as well as Construction Charges.

: Sales Invoice with Measurements and Construction Charges

Escape back to the Job Card.

Add New Measurements

As previously stated, invoices are created through the planning process for all measurements until all positions have been invoiced. This section shows how to add measurements.

In the Job Card, click Measurement Document. A message appears asking to insert the complete BoQ in the measurement document.

: Creating a new Measurement Document for the job

Click Yes to insert the complete BoQ. A new Measurement Recording Card opens.

: New Measurement Recording Card for job

The process of updating the Measurement Recording Card is the same as described in topic .

Populate the Measurement Date, Measurement Page No. and Measurement Location fields, being sure to update the lines as well.

: Measurement fields updated in header and lines

In the Lines FastTab, enter the measured quantities in the Quantity field. If any new measurements were taken on registered quantities, the Cum. Quantity field will show the cumulative total. For example, three lines have registered quantities (4, 4, and 7, respectively). New totals are entered for all three lines (5, 4, and 6, respectively) and the Cum. Quantity field shows the cumulative total of the new Quantity and Cum. Registered Quantity (9, 8, and 13, respectively).

: New measurements added for registered positions and new positions

Click Register to register the measurement and then click Yes to register the document and OK to close the message that the document has been registered. The Measurement Recording Card closes.

Create Second Invoice

Now that new measurements have been added to the job, the next invoice can be created using the same process described previously.

Click Sale to open the Job Budget Sales page. Click Actions > Job Invoicing to access the planning functions.

: Preparing to create the next Sales Invoice

First click Planning to run the sales planning process, then click Release to create the Sales Invoice. In the Job Invoicing Release dialog, enter the correct posting date. The Sales Invoice field is updated to indicate the invoice was created.

: Job Invoicing Planning and Release functions completed

Click Yes in the Sales Invoice field to open the Sales Invoice. The invoice includes new and previously invoiced positions, and the new Measurement Summary Code appears in the Construction Invoicing FastTab.

: Second sales invoice with new and previously invoiced lines

Notice that the registered measurement quantities appear in the lines.

Click Navigate > Measurement Summary to view the cumulative posting. Notice that the cumulative totals appear in the summary.

: Cumulative and new quantities in the Measurement Summary

Click OK to close the Measurement Summary page.

In the Construction Invoicing FastTab, update the Period of performance from and Period of performance to fields.

: Period of performance updated in sales invoice

Add Construction Charges to Invoice

On the Navigate tab, click CS Invoice Calculation. The CS Invoicings page opens.

: Running the CS Invoice Calculation function

Click Edit to open the Invoicing page, change the Status to Released and then click Calculate.

: Invoicing released and ready for calculation

The Value and Cum. Value fields are calculated. Click Transfer Values to open the Calculation Value Transfer page.

: Calculation Value Transfer page with calculated values

Select both charges and then select the last line in the Transfer to Line(s) FastTab.

: Both charge lines to be transferred below the last invoice line

Click Transfer Values to complete the transfer process.

: Charges have been transferred below the last invoice line

Click OK to close the Calculation Value Transfer and Invoicing pages, click Close to close the CS Invoicings page.

In the Sales Invoice, notice that the charges have been added below the last sales line.

: Charges transferred to Sales Invoice

Post Sales Invoice and Print Document

Click Home > Post to post the sales invoice. After posting, click Yes to open the Posted Sales Invoice.

Click Actions > Print to open the Sales - Invoice dialog (for report 5292686). A new Saved Settings FastTab appears indicating that the last used options and filters have been set.

: Sales - Invoice print dialog with new Saved Settings FastTab

Click Print to generate a document or Preview to view the document. The Sales Invoice document contains the cumulative quantities for invoiced/new and new measurements, as well as Construction Charges.

: Sales Invoice with Measurements and Construction Charges

Escape back to the Job Card.

The above processes must be completed until all invoice planning lines have been invoiced.
