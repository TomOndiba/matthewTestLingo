Create Measurement Recording

After a new Measurement is created and a number is assigned, enter the job in the Job No. field and then press ENTER or TAB to validate the field. A message appears asking to insert the complete BoQ from the job into the measurement recording.

: Measurement page after Job No. input

Click Yes to insert the complete BoQ, or No to only add the job information in the header.

: Measurement Recording updated with job information in header and BoQ positions in lines

In the Measurement Date field on the General FastTab, enter the date of the measurement recording and then press ENTER or TAB to validate the field. A message appears asking to update all lines with the measurement date.

: Message to update lines with Measurement Date entered in header

Click Yes to update the date in the lines, or No to only add the date to the header.

: Measurement date added to header and all lines

In the Measurement Page No.field, enter a number to represent the page number, e.g., 001 and then press ENTER or TAB to validate the field. A message appears asking to update all lines with the measurement page number.

: Message to update lines with Page No. entered in header

Click Yes to update the Page No. in the lines, or No to only add the Page No. to the header.

: Measurement Page No. added to header and all lines

Measurement locations are specific to each job so will need to be set up for each Measurement Recording. In the Measurement Location field, click the arrow to open the list of locations and then click New.

: Creating a Measurement Location

In the Measurement Locations page, enter a code and description for the location. The No. of Measurement Lines and Measurement Amount fields will be automatically populated after application to the Measurement Recording.

: Measurement Locations

Click OK to close the Measurement Locations page and assign to the Measurement. Press ENTER or TAB to validate the field. A message appears asking to update all lines with the measurement location.

: Message to update lines with Measurement Location entered in header

Click Yes to update the Measurement Location in the lines, or No to only add the Measurement Location to the header.

Notice that the Measurement Location is updated in the header (and in the lines if the Management Location field is shown).

: Measurement Location added to the header

On the relevant line(s), enter the changed values in the Quantity field.

: Changed measurement quantities
