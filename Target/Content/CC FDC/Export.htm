Export

The export integrates the cc|factory data capture into standard Microsoft Dynamics NAV. Therefore “Output” or “Item Positive/Negative Adjm.” gets posted depending on their source. The FDC Export Queue lists all FDC Entries that haven’t been exported yet. The export may be run automatically after each posting (Automatic Export When Posting field in FDC Setup) or manually using the FDC Export Queue.

A cumulative export combines multiple FDC Detailed Entries with the same source and posts an aggregated Journal Line. Therefore, the amount of entries in standard Microsoft Dynamics NAV can be reduced, compared to the number of FDC Entries. The combined Time-Quantity-Export summarizes additional “Recording” or “Output” FDC Entries.
