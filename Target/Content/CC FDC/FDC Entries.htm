FDC Entries

An FDC Entry aggregates a complete presence and absence, and consists of at least one corresponding FDC Detailed Entry. Multiple FDC Detailed Entries for one FDC Entry enable processing of different interruptions and recordings in just one FDC Entry. So the FDC Entry finally represents the Net-, Gross- and Interruption Time and combines multiple lines that were entered in the Input Journal. The FDC Detailed Entries function provides more details about an FDC Entry.

Additionally, an FDC Entry can be of Entry Type = Output or Consumption. The underlying FDC Detailed Entries only consist of the Entry Type = Recording and contain the corresponding quantities.

The Gross Time (h) field corresponds to the sum of Recording, Setup and Idle. The Net Time (h) field is deducted by Breaks and Unplanned Failure Time.
