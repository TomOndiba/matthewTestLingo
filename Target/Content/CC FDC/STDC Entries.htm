STDC Entries

The structure of STDC entries contains, just like FDC Entries, STDC Entries and underlying STDC Detailed Entries. A STDC Entry aggregates full presence and absence, and consists of at least one according STDC Detailed Entry. Multiple STDC Detailed Entries of an STDC Entry allow different Interruptions and Presence Times (“Recording”) to be processed in a single STDC Entry. Therefore, an STDC Entry represents the Net Time, Gross Time and Interruption Time and combines multiple lines that were entered into the Input Journal into just one line. The STDC Detailed Entries function provides more details about an STDC Entry.

The Gross Time corresponds to the sum of recording. In the Net Time field, breaks and unplanned failure times get deducted.
