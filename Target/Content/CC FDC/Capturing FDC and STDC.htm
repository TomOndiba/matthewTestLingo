Capturing (FDC and STDC)

Times and quantities in cc|factory data capture can be captured in the following ways:

  • FDC Application Capturing (RoleTailored Client FDC Terminal)
  • FDC Dialogs (cc|mobile solution)
  • FDC Input Journal
  • FDC Journal
  • STDC Journal

The different journals are accessible from the navigation pane by clicking Departments > COSMO CONSULT > cc|bde (FDC) > Planning & Execution. The respective entries can be accessed by clicking Departments > COSMO CONSULT > cc|bde (FDC) > History.

FDC Application Capturing – which contains the Input Journal, the FDC Terminal and the FDC Dialogs – is the easiest way to capture times and quantities using timestamps and different types (check in, check out).
