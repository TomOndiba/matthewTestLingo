Calculation Lines Chart

On the Lines FastTab, under Functions, another Chart option exists. This option can be filtered on non-totaling Calculation lines by selecting the lines in the calculation and then running the “Chart” function. To keep these lines filtered, do not change the settings on the ribbon.

For example, to look at the Cost price and Target retail price breakdown for the item, select the calculation lines and then click Functions > Chart.
