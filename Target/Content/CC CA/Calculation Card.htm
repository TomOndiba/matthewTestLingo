Calculation Card

New or existing calculations are displayed in the Calculation Card page, which is opened from list views in the following:

  • Sales & Marketing menu by clicking Inventory & Pricing > Calculations
  • Items list page or Item Card by clicking Calculation on the ribbon
  • Sales Order line by clicking Lines FastTab > Line > Calculation
  • Production Order line by clicking Lines FastTab > Line > Calculation (or Calculation (Expected) for non-released production orders)
  • Assembly Orders by clicking Calculation (Expected) or Calculation (Actual) on the ribbon

Note

Calculations on sales lines, production order lines and Assembly Orders are described in topic “Calculations for Document Lines” on page 1.
