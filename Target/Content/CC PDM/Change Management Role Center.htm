Change Management: Role Center

As soon as a change order with associated tasks has been created, it is displayed in the Role Center of the PDM Manager. There, the entire content is divided into three Cue Groups in the Activities part:

  • Eng. Change Notices (ECNs) – These Cues show the number of change notices sent from Production or Item Management to the Product Data Manager.

Note

Open orders should be checked and processed by the PDM manager so that there is no delay.

  • Eng. Change Orders (ECOs) – These Cues show the number of change orders and the status of each of these change orders at the current time.

Note

Ready to Finish means that this change order must still be finished by the PDM manager.

  • Eng. Change Order Tasks – These Cues indicate the extent to which processing was carried out by the PDM manager. Due and overdue changes should be reviewed and processed.
