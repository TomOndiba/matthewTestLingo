Sales Order Manufacturing Preview View

The Requested Delivery Date on the Sales Order is used as the Due Date in the preview. On the ribbon of the Sales Order, click Actions > GX Manufacturing Preview.

Note

The Requested Delivery Date field must be populated on the Sales Order to use this feature.

: Sales Preview for order with Shipment Date of January 16, 2017 and Requested Delivery Date of January 18, 2017

The first Sales Preview row shows the production order for the sales order as of the Shipment Date and links to the second Sales Preview row which ends on the Requested Delivery Date (Due Date). No changes to the sales order/production order can be made in this view; it is only a method of viewing other production orders that could be preventing a specific delivery date.

The temporary planned production order is deleted when the Sales Order Manuf. Preview page is closed.
