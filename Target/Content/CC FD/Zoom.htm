Zoom

The view can be increased to display a small part of content in more detail or decreased to see a bigger area of content.

: Zoom

Icons

To increase or decrease the view by using icons, follow these steps:

  1. Click the icon in the status bar of the Text Editor.
  2. In the opened submenu, click to increase the view or click to decrease the view.

: Zoom Submenu

Mouse Wheel

To increase or decrease the view by using the mouse wheel, follow these steps:

  1. To increase or decrease the view, press the CTRL key.
  2. Move the mouse wheel forward to increase or backward to decrease the view. Keep the CTRL key pressed while moving the mouse wheel.
