Spell Checking

Spell checking in cc|formatted documents is based on the free software “Hunspell”, which is also used by other software like OpenOffice, or products of Adobe or Mozilla. Hunspell is downward compatible to MySpell Dictionaries. There are more than 90 languages available at the OpenOffice website. Beside the OpenOffice website there are several more sites offering Hunspell Dictionaries.

These dictionaries can be downloaded in a desired language and stored in the installation folder of cc|formatted documents. cc|formatted documents recognizes available dictionaries in the subfolder “Hunspell” automatically. Hunspell Dictionaries can be downloaded from the following websites:

  • OpenOffice Dictionaries: http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/en/dictionaries
  • Mozilla Firefox Dictionaries: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/language-tools

Change Language of Content

The active language is displayed in the status bar of the Text Editor.

: Language in Status Bar of the Text Editor

To change the language of the content, follow these steps:

  1. Click the action . The Spell Check page will open.
  2. Choose the new language from the dropdown list and then close the Spell Check page.
