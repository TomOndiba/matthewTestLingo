Insert Content from Other Sources

Content can be inserted in the word processor from the internet or from other programs using the clipboard (Copy & Paste). The source formatting is used as much as possible, however prohibited formatting will be removed and possibly the entire contents will be altered. If markup is invalid, the related content may not be pasted into the word processor.

A warning will be generated if the content was changed by the application.

: Content Adjustment by Word Processing

If content with its formatting cannot be inserted or this method leads to unusable results, use the "Paste as Text" option in the START FastTab in the Clipboard group below the button symbol to remove any source formatting.
