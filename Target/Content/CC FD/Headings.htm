Headings

A heading highlights text and assists readers in finding the required information.

: Headings

To format text as a heading, follow these steps:

  1. Mark the text to be changed.
  2. Click the action in the START tab in the Formatting group.
  3. Choose between six different levels to structure the text as needed. Level 1 is the highest, while Level 6 is the lowest.

Reset Heading

To format a text as a normal font or reset a heading, follow these steps:

  1. Mark the text to be changed.
  2. Click the action in the START tab in the Formatting group.
  3. Choose Normal from the list.
