Insert Images

To insert an image from a file, follow these steps:

  1. Click where the image should be inserted.
  2. Click the action in the INSERT tab in the Graphics group.
  3. Browse to the location of the image to insert.
  4. Double-click the image or choose it and apply the selection by clicking Open.

: Images

Note

Images are embedded by cc|formatted documents. The size of an image must not exceed 2 MB.
