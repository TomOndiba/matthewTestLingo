Example – Formula Quantity

In this example, the variables “VAR1” and “VAR2” in the Master BOM are multiplied with one another to calculate the necessary square meters.

: Formula Quantity

In the checklist template, the two variables are defined for “Length” and “Width” calculations. When values are entered in the Input fields on the document checklist and the recalculation is run, the results are transferred to the Value of Function fields on the function lines.

: Document Checklist with calculated values

When the master BOM is opened, the calculated value from the checklist is transferred to the Exp. Quantity field on the relevant Document BOM line.

: Formula Quantity results in Document BOM
