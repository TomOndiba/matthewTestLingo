Sales Quote

In the Sales Quote, order-related BOMs and checklists can be added on a sales line basis. This means that it is possible to include an item several times in the sales lines, but each time with a different specification. When a configurable item is entered, the assigned checklist template is automatically copied and is available as a “document checklist”.
