Additional Service Functions

cc|text module functionality has been added to the Service Item Worksheet. This can be used to enter additional information during the service (e.g. why a part was replaced). This text will be transferred to the Service BOM so that other users know the reason of the exchange. It improves the traceability of the service process. Refer to topic “cc|text module” on page 1 for general information about cc|text module.

Checklists can also be assigned on the Service Order header. To create a checklist from a Service Order, click Actions > Create Checklist on the ribbon.
