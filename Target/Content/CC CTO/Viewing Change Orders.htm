Viewing Change Orders

An overview of all change orders is accessible from the navigation pane by clicking cc|configure to order > Order Processing.

Individual change orders can be viewed from the following source documents by clicking Open Change Order on the Actions tab:

  • Item Card
  • Production BOM
  • Document BOM / Document BOM Header
  • Production Orders, General FastTab (planned, firm planned and released)

: Change Order access and indicator on Item Card

The number of change orders assigned to a source document is indicated in the No. of Change Orders field as follows:

  • Item Card, Config. to Order FastTab
  • Production BOM, General FastTab
  • Document BOM, Document BOM Details FactBox (Change Orders field)
  • Document BOM Header, General FastTab
  • Production Orders, General FastTab (planned, firm planned and released)

Clicking the value in this field opens the Change Orders page from where existing orders also can be opened.
