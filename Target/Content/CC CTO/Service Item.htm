Service Item

If a service item – which was configured using cc|configure to order – is generated by the system, some fields on the Service Item Card are filled in automatically. The BOMs, production orders and checklists for the production of the service items are entered in the Document BOM No., Construction Order No., Prod. Order No. and Document Checklist No. fields on the Detail FastTab. To open these documents, use the arrow in these fields or click Navigate > Production History on the ribbon and then select one of the menu options.

A checklist specific to a service item can be opened by clicking Navigate > Create Service Item Checklist on the ribbon. The system will ask which Checklist Template is to be used. The Checklist No. will be linked in the Service Item Checklist field. In the Active Service BOM field, the number of the assigned BOM is displayed.
