Example – Price Calculation

In this example, an item has a sales price of 3500.00 EUR according to the sales line. On the Calculation FastTab, a 10% discount is entered.

The system first calculates the 10% discount of the 3500.00 EUR and determines a calculation price of 3150.00 EUR.

: Example Price Calculation via Checklist

The Frame Type option of City - Senior has an additional charge of 50 EUR. On this amount, a 5% discount is granted since this option is set up for additional discounts. That means, the 10% discount on the Calculation FastTab is used in addition to the line discount: 50 EUR - 2.50 EUR (5%) - 5 EUR (10%) = 42.50 EUR. This amount will be added to the calculation price of 3150.00 EUR as the net additional charge and a new calculation price of 3,192.50 EUR is calculated when the “Calculate Price” function is run.

: Price calculation including City - Senior frame

The Frame Height option of 55 has a gross additional charge of 20 EUR. On that line, a 10% discount (2 EUR) is granted. Since this option is not set up for additional discounts, only the 18 EUR net discount is added to the calculation price. When the “Calculate Price” function is run, the result is 3,210.50 EUR, as indicated in the Calculation Price field on the Calculation FastTab.

: Price calculation including frame height of 55

The Frame Height option of 55 has an additional weight of 5 kg, which is added to the item’s current net weight of 12 kg. Therefore, the Calculation Weight field on the Calculation FastTab is calculated to 17 kg when the “Calculate Price” function is run.

: Weight calculation including additional weight for frame height of 55

New price calculations are activated for changes in the lines by clicking Calculate Price on the ribbon (or pressing CTRL+F9). To recalculate formulas only, click Recalculate on the ribbon (or press F9). To transfer the calculated sales price and net weight to the sales line, click Transfer Unit Price on the ribbon (or press CTRL+S).
