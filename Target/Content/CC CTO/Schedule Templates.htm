Schedule Templates

Schedule Templates provide the basic setup for the operation schedules.

: Schedule Template

Once set up, the Schedule Templates can be assigned to a sales/purchase order by clicking Line > Document Schedule on the Lines FastTab.

: Selecting for a Schedule Template for a sales line

On the ribbon, click Edit to open the Schedule Template page.

The standard schedules created are recorded in chronological order. The standard values can be changed here. A person responsible for these can also be assigned here. Deadlines can be recorded both sequentially and in parallel using the Previous Operation No. and Next Operation No. fields.
