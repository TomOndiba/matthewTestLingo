Construction Order

In cc|configure to order, a company’s engineering or construction department can use the construction order for managing the BOM prior to manufacturing. Therefore, the construction order is considered the work order for the construction department. The design engineer has the option of modifying the document BOM created by sales or creating a new BOM. In this case, he or she has access to the checklist. The design engineer is also optionally supported by the PDM system “ProfiDB” here.
