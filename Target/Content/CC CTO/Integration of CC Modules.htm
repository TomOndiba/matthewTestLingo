Integration of CC Modules

The usage of production structures for BOM configuration capabilities of cc|process manufacturing and the extended text capabilities of cc|text module have been integrated into cc|configure to order to provide greater configuration of BOMs and flexibility in producing documents from Microsoft Dynamics NAV.

  • With cc|process manufacturing, users have the ability to create items and production structures from configurations and multilevel BOMs.
  • With cc|text module, users have the ability to add more information to checklists, master/document/construction BOMs, which can then be inserted as header and footer texts to be included in sales and purchase documents, as well as to transfer relevant configuration information from the sales document to the construction order.

The functionalities of the above modules that are specific to cc|configure to order are described in this topic.
