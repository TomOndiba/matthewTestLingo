Service BOM

To create or view a BOM for a service item, click either the AssistEdit button in the Active Service BOM field or click Navigate > Service BOM on the ribbon.

The Service BOM is similar to a construction order in its structure and usage but differs in that the line information can be entered directly in the Service BOM without opening another page.
