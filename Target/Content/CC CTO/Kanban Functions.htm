Kanban Functions

The following functions are available from the Home and/or the Navigate tabs.

  • Register Card: Increases the number in the Collected Quantity field.
  • Undo Card: Decreases the number in the Collected Quantity field.
  • Make Documents: Creates Requisition Lines, a Purchase Order, or a Production Order if the Collected Quantity field is greater than zero.
  • Print: Generates Kanban Cards. On the Options FastTab of the request page, indicate how many cards are needed. The system defaults the quantity specified in the Total No. of Kanban Cards field on the KANBAN Setup page.
  • Supply: Opens an overview of any existing supply.
