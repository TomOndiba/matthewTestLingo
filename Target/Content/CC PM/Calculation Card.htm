Calculation Card

New or existing calculations are displayed in the Calculation Card page, which in addition to the standard cc|calculation list views, can be opened from these cc|process manufacturing list views:

  • Manufacturing menu by clicking Research & Development or Product Design > Calculations
  • Manufacturing / Production Structures list page or Manufacturing / Production Structure card by clicking Calculation on the ribbon

: cc|process manufacturing fields on Calculation Card

The General FastTab of the Calculation Card contains two fields that are specific to cc|process manufacturing. Both are described in the following table.

Field

Description

Recipe Weight

Defaults from the Item Card.

Production Structure No.

The production structure must be certified or a BOM or routing must be included.

If a new production structure is selected, the production BOM and routing numbers will be populated. The BOM and routing are the basis for the calculation.
