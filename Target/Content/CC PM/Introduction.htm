Introduction

Built to enhance Microsoft Dynamics® NAV with industry-specific functionality, COSMO CONSULT offers an industry-specific solution with “cc|industry solution: process manufacturing” that is geared specifically for plastics processing and the manufacture of chemicals, paints and varnishes. This solution supports business processes ranging from sales through production with complete integration with the finance system.

Traditionally sold as independent solutions and add-on modules, cc|industry solution: process manufacturing offers a packaged set of functionality that delivers support for:

  • Managing the blending, mixing, reacting and by-product/co-product operations required by process manufacturers
  • Handling the fabrication and assembly steps needed by discrete and lean manufacturers
  • Providing the requirements demanded by highly regulated environments

cc|industry solution: process manufacturing (herein after referred to as cc|process manufacturing) includes management and development of recipes, efficient production planning and sequencing, a quality management system as well as forward/reverse batch tracing and container management. The scope of delivery can be supplemented by an optional management of hazardous goods and automotive module. An optional workflow module aids process design and documentation.

Developed in close coordination with the industry, all functions of cc|process manufacturing represent proven best practices. This solution for the process manufacturer provides scalable process control and standardized methods for smaller manufacturers to divisions of larger enterprises.

Key Features

  • Sequence Planning
  • Recipe Management
  • Production Structure
  • By-production
  • Flexible Calculation
  • Production Simulation
  • Quality Management
  • Maintenance/Setup Jobs
