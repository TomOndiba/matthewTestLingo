Contingent Management for a Customer Contact

Sample shipments can be created for a customer contact directly in the Sales Order. Within the order, samples and regular items can be combined freely.

: Sales order with contingent item

To ship an item that has been set up as a contingent item, the item number must be entered in the No. field on the sales line. The system automatically copies the item’s Contingent Type to the Contingent Type field on the line (this field is not automatically displayed on the line). If no contingent type has been entered on the Item Card, the user can manually enter or choose an appropriate type. The combination of item number and contingent type is used to check the Contingent Group (entered on the Contact Card) to determine if the maximum quantity to be shipped has been exceeded.

: Maximum quantity shipped message

When the quantity or the contingent type is entered, the system checks the following conditions:

  • Is the item released for contingents?
  • Is the contact assigned to a contingent group?
  • Is the item released for the contingent type in the contingent group?
  • Is the quantity in the sales line plus the quantity that has already been shipped within the predefined period of time less than the maximum quantity being specified in the setup?

If this is not the case, the system will display an error message (refer to Fig. 2). Here, the maximum permitted quantity, the shipped quantity within the time interval, the remaining quantity in other un-posted sales orders and the quantity of the current document are displayed.

  • The system uses the Shipment Date from the sales header as the reference date.
