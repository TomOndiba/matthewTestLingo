Exercise – Production Journal

In the Released Production Order for 30 units of item P-10002, post only 20 units of output in the Production Journal. Then review the Sequence Planning to ensure that scheduling is using remaining quantities rather than the full production quantity.

Note

To complete this exercise, first post an item journal for 500 units each of items P-30008 and P-30009 into the MAINWH location on January 1, 2019.

Post Output in the Production Journal and Review Sequence Planning

Follow these steps to post output in the Production Journal and review Sequence Planning:

  1. In the navigation pane, under Manufacturing, click Planning > Sequence Planning.
  2. In the General section, select the M002 work center line.
  3. Locate the production order for 30 units of item P-10002 (created in the Sequence Planning exercise. If not completed, create a released production order for 30 units of item P-10002 to the MAINWH location).
  4. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Production Journal.
  5. In the header, change the value in the Posting Quantity field to 20.
  6. On the ribbon, click Post and then click Yes to post the journal lines.
  7. Click OK to close the posting confirmation.
  8. Click OK to close the Production Journal.
  9. On the Sequence Planning line, notice that the Quantity is 30 but the Remaining Quantity is 10.

Sequence planning is using the remaining quantity, not the quantity. When you post some but not all output, sequence is recalculated using the remaining quantity.
