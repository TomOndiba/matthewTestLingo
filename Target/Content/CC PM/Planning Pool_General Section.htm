General Section

In the General section, users can set several filters that affect the production orders listed on the lines. In these filters, wild cards can be used, for example, Item Filter = P* means that all items beginning with the letter P will appear.

Method of Production Filter

The Method of Production Filter field on the Planning Pool page allows users to filter planning lines by the method of production, which is specified on the item cards (refer to Fig. 1). This filter helps separate production orders from the different production areas.
