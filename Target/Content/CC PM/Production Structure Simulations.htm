Production Structure Simulations

The Production Structure Simulation calculates a certain quantity for the needed work operations and materials. This is done in several steps. If there are items in the structure that are also produced as semi-finished goods, they are exploded in their structure.

This function is accessible from the Production Structure, Item Card and Sales Order.

: Highlighting the Production Simulation function on the Production Structure

From the Production Structure and/or Item Card, multilevel simulations are possible.

: Production Simulation from the Production Structure

The Production Simulation takes the quantity to calculate from the Lot Size field on the Production Structure and Item Card. This quantity can be changed on the item card and the production structure.

The Due Date is taken from the work date.
