Work Center Production Rates

A tool determines the Work Center Production Rates and Setup Time of the machine using the following formulas:

  • Calculation of performance: Cavity * (Cycle Quantity / Cycle Time)
  • Calculation of setup time: Ramp-up time + Tool Setup Time

The information entered on the Tool FastTab of the Tool Work Center Card is transferred to the Tool/Insert FastTab on the Work Center Prod. Rate Card page to calculate the performance and setup times.
