QC Status Maintenance

QC Statuses are maintained in the QC Status Change page. In the header, filters can be set on various item-related fields. To search, click Find on the ribbon. Once the data is found, the QC Status on individual lines can be changed by entering the new status in the New QC Status field, selecting those lines and then clicking Change on the ribbon.

: QC Status changed from CHECK to FREE on two entries of item P-958-QC001

Alternatively, run the “Batch QC Status Change” function to change the status based on conditions.
