Item Delivery

If receipt testing is not used, ingredients can be entered on incoming inventory (for example, through the Item Journal or Purchase Order) during the item tracking process. In the Item Tracking Lines page, click Navigate > Lot No. Information Card. Click New to open the relevant card and then click Navigate > Ingredients.

: Lot No. Ingredients opened from Lot No. Information Card in Item Tracking Lines

In the Lot No. Ingredients page, assign the actual ingredient quantities and select the Actual Value check box, click OK and then complete the item tracking and receipt process.
