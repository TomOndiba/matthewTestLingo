Recipe Statistics

In the Recipe Statistics, the system compares expected and actual quantity, as well as expected net weight and actual net weight. It also displays information about the expected and actual raw material in base unit of measure and in percentages.

The Prod. Order Recipe Statistics page is accessible directly from the Research & Development menu or from a Finished Production Order.

From the Research & Development menu, production order information must be entered in the header.

: Production order recipe statistics from Research & Development menu

To open the page from a finished production order, on the Lines FastTab, click Line > Recipe Statistics. The fields are filled in automatically with the production order information.

Note

The Prod. Order Recipe Statistics page is also accessible from the released production order line.
