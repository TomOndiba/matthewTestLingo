Combine Trading Unit Administration for Status Change

The “Combine Trading Unit Administration” function changes the Status for all Trading Units of a Lot in one step in theLot-/Serial No. Administration page. If the Combined Trading Unit Administration field is activated in Quality Management Setup, then an extra record will be created for the lot with an empty Trading Unit No. in the Lot No. Information table.

: Combined Trading Unit Administration field activated in Quality Management Setup

On the extra record in the Lot-/Serial No. Administration page, the status change is entered for the entire lot quantity.

: Extra record with empty trading unit, Quantity for full Lot No. Inventory and New Status entered

When the entry is posted, the status of each trading unit is updated with the selection entered in the empty record.

: Status of each trading unit updated from one posted line
