Restricted Purchases

Purchasing restrictions (Restricted Purchase) can refer to items or purchasing products, which may or should only be purchased from certain vendors.

Purchasing restrictions are created on the Item Card from the Navigate tab by clicking Vendors.

: Item Vendor Catalog for item P-50004

In the Item Vendor Catalog page, enter the relevant vendor in the Vendor No. field and then select the Approved check box to restrict the purchase to this vendor.

Purchases are then restricted to approved vendors by selecting the Use Only Approved Vendors check box on the Replenishment FastTab of the Item Card. When selected, the respective item can only be purchased from vendors that have been entered and approved in the Item Vendor Catalog.

: Approved vendor setting on Item Card

If a purchase order is created for a vendor with assigned purchase restriction, the respective item can be entered in the purchase line. If the respective item number is entered in the purchase line for a vendor who has not been assigned to the restricted purchase, a warning message is displayed that the vendor is not approved for this item.
