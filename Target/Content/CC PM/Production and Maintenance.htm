Production and Maintenance Planning

The Adjustment Maintenance page (Plant Maintenance > Adjustment Maintenance) is used for the coordination of production planning and maintenance planning. This page displays all maintenance orders that have been created along with the relevant production order information and allows users to open the maintenance order and the sequence planning to process planning corrections.

: Adjustment Maintenance page

Select a line and then click View on the ribbon to open the Adjust. Maintenance Card page, which provides a better overview of the comparison.

: Adjust. Maintenance Card for maintenance order W-A-003

If the production planner and the maintenance planner agree upon a date, it is confirmed by using the “Copy Starting Date from Production Order” function. That means that the production information is transferred to the maintenance order (and not from the maintenance order to the production order). The Coordinated check box is selected after confirmation.

On the maintenance order, the Manufacturing FastTab contains all the relevant data, such as if a production order is fixed in sequence, and starting/ending dates.

Once there is a change in the production planning or maintenance order, the Coordinated check box is cleared and the confirmation process must begin again.
