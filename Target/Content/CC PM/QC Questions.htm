QC Questions

In the QC Question Card page, enter standard questions that can be selected in the QC Templates lines. Refer to online help for a description of the fields on this page.

: QC Question Card, related answers, and the QC Answer Card for the QC-A-001 answer

Possible answers can be assigned to the questions that can be transferred to the QC Template when the question is assigned in the lines.

On the QC Question Card, click Navigate > Answers to open the QC Question/Answer Combi. page. In this page, the answers are assigned to the questions.
