Customers and Locations

Customer Locations are created and assigned to the customer in order to transfer returnable containers using the Item Reclass. Journal instead of creating an item ledger entry by posting a sales shipment. During delivery, the customer location for the returnable container is booked as a transfer location and records which customer received which returnable container.

Location Card

Select the Customer Location check box on the Location Card to designate a location as a customer location. Only locations with this designation can be selected on the Customer Card.

Customer Card

Assign the customer location on the Shipping FastTab of the Customer Card.
