Delivery Tolerances and Prices

When pricing is based on quantities sold/purchased, it can be affected by the change due to delivery tolerances. This situation is managed by the Calculate New Price field in the Delivery Tolerances page. If the field is set to Always, On Overdelivery or On Underdelivery, the price of the document line must be recalculated in consideration of the new quantity on entering of an over-/under-delivery. If the field is set to No, the unit price/cost remains unchanged.
