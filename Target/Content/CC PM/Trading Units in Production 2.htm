Trading Units in Production

Items can be produced in units of measure other than the base unit of measure. This can be done by specifying the unit of measure that will be transferred to the production order in the Production Unit field on the Item Card.

: Production Unit options on the Item Card

There are two options in the Production Unit field:

  • Base Unit – Production orders for this item are created in base unit of measure.
  • Order Unit – Productions orders that are based on sales orders are created in the unit of measure determined in the sales order.
