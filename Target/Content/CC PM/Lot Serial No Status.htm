Lot-/Serial No. Status

In addition to setting filters on Customers and Countries in the Lot-/Serial No. Status page, it is possible to set filters on Customer Groups. The settings of the Color Code field can also be used on Ingredient Management pages in cc|process manufacturing.

: Lot-/Serial No. Status page

These fields are described in the following table.

Field

Description

Customer Group Filter

Customer Group that the status relates to. The filter entered here is considered at the following places within the application:

  • Planning – Inventory that was released for certain customer groups is only considered for the fulfillment of demand of orders by customers that belong to this customer group. For all other customers, this inventory is considered as not available in planning.
  • Picking – Inventory that was released for certain customer groups is only used in the picking order if the respective sales order was created for a customer belonging to this customer group.
  • Item Availability Check in the Sales Order – When item availability is checked in the Sales Order, only inventory where the deposited customer group filter at the status matches the customer group of the customer in the sales header is viewed as available. Inventory where the customer group filter of the status does not match the customer group of the customer in the sales header cannot be entered in the item tracking line.

Furthermore, the shipment posting is denied if the customer group filter of the status does not match the customer group of the customer in the sales header (such as if the status was altered after entering the item tracking line).

Color Code

Indicates a color to provide visualization to the Status field on the Ingredient Lot Overview and Ingredient Lot - Select pages.
