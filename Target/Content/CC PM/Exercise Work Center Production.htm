Exercise – Work Center Production Rates

On the Production Structure for item P-10002, delete the Setup Time and Run Time for work center M002 and then certify the Production Structure.

On the Item Card, add the item unit of measure of KG. Set up the Work Center Production Rates for machine M002 with a capacity of 60 kg per hour and two hours of setup time.

Manually create a firm planned production order for item P-10002 and confirm the time in the routing.

Update the Work Center on the Production Structure

Follow these steps to update the work center on the production structure:

  1. In the navigation pane, under Manufacturing, click Product Design > Production Structures.
  2. Open the production structure for item P-10002.
  3. Change the Status to Under Development.
  4. On the line for Work Center M002, remove the values in the Setup Time and Run Time fields.
  5. Change the Status to Certified.
  6. Do not close the Production Structure page.

Add Unit of Measure and Production Rates to Item P-10002

Follow these steps to add the unit of measure and production rates on the Item Card:

  1. In the Production Structure Details FactBox, click Item No. P-10002 to open the Item Card.
  2. Click Navigate > Units of Measure.
  3. On the ribbon, click New.
  4. In the Code field, enter KG.
  5. In the Qty. per Unit of Measure field, type .04.
  6. Click Yes to change the Net Weight and then click OK to close the Item Units of Measure page.
  7. On the Navigate tab, click Production > Work Center Production Rates.
  8. On the ribbon, click New.
  9. In the Work Center No. field, enter M002.
  10. In the Setup Time field, type 2.
  11. In the Setup Time Unit of Measure field, enter HOURS.
  12. In the Production Rate field, type 60.
  13. In the Prod. Rate Unit of Measure field, enter KG.
  14. In the Production Rate Time field, type 1.
  15. In the Prod. Rate per UOM field, enter HOURS.
  16. Click OK to close the open pages.

Create the Production Order and Verify Setup

Follow these steps to create the production order and verify the routing setup:

  1. In the navigation pane, under Manufacturing, click Execution > Firm Planned Prod. Orders.
  2. On the ribbon, click New.
  3. Press ENTER to insert a firm planned production order number.
  4. In the Source No. field, enter P-10002.
  5. In the Quantity field, type 1.
  6. Expand the Posting FastTab and enter MAINWH in the Location Code field.
  7. On the ribbon, click Refresh Production Order.
  8. In the Refresh Production Order batch job, click OK.
  9. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Routing.

: Routing times created from Production Rate Time

  1. Confirm that the Setup Time and Run Time fields are calculated properly as set up in the Work Center Prod. Rate Card.
  2. Close the open pages.
