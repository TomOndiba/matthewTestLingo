List of Shipped Contingents

The list of shipped contingents can either be accessed from the Contact Card or Customer Card from the Shipped Contingents action on the Navigate tab.

: Shipped Contingents action from the contact card

In the Posted Sales Shipment Lines page, the system displays all sales shipments (Contingent Type <> blank) for the respective contact or customer.

: Shipped Contingents for contact CT000302

The related sales shipment can be accessed by clicking Navigate > Show Document.
