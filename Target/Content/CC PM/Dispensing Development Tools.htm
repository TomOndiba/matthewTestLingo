Dispensing Development Tools

The four tools available to assist in the development of recipes are:

  • Check Recipe
  • Recipe Comparison
  • Recipe Search
  • Content Specification

The Check Recipe tool can be opened from any of the production structures while the Recipe Comparison and Recipe Search tools can be opened from any of the production structures, the recipe card, or directly from the Research & Development or Product Design menus. The Content Specification is only available in the Development Structure page.
