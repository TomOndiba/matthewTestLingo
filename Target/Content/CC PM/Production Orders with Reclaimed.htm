Production Orders with Reclaimed Items

When creating a production order, the reclaimed item will be calculated into a new production line.

: Production order calculated with separate reclaimed item line

As Fig. 1 illustrates, 100 pieces of item P-950-RG001 are to be produced. The net weight of this item is 16 kg, accordingly 1600 kg. With a scrap of 10%, the reclaimed item P-951-RG002 is calculated with 160 kg and entered as a new production line.
