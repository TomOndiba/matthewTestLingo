Production Structure Number

The Production Structure No. field is located on the Replenishment FastTab. This field identifies the production structure for this item. It can be entered manually or inserted automatically when it is certified. In addition, the system also inserts the related Routing No. and Production BOM No. / Recipe No. This field allows users to assign one Production Structure for multiple items.

Refer to topic “Production Structure Setup” on page 1 for more information.
