Production BOM Types

On the Replenishment FastTab on the Item Card, the Production BOM Type field determines if a production BOM or recipe is used:

  • If Recipe is selected, a Recipe No. can be entered or selected in the Production BOM No. field.
  • If the field is empty, a standard production BOM can be entered or selected in the Production BOM No. field.
