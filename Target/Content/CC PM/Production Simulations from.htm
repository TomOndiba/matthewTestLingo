Production Simulations from Sales Orders

Production Simulations can be run from Sales Orders. In the Sales Order, the quantities and dates are based on the sales line Quantity and the Shipment Date.

: Sales Order preparing to run the Production Simulation

In this example, an order is placed for 20 200L barrels of the item P-10502-F200, Dipping Varnish White, with the Shipment Date of January 29, 2019.

: Production Simulation from the Sales Order

The time and material calculation is based on the Due Date of January 29, 2019, the Quantity of 20 and the Unit of Measure of F-200. Fig. 2 illustrates that a multilevel structure is possible.

The Warning check box is selected if there are problems with the Quantity Available on Due Date or with the capacity of a Work Center.
