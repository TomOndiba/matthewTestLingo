Items

Select the Returnable Container check box on the Warehouse FastTab to designate an item as a returnable container.

: Returnable Container check box on the Item Card

For items that need returnable containers, the information is captured in the Item Returnable Containers page, accessed from the Item Card by clicking Navigate > Returnable Containers.

: Returnable containers assigned to item P-10600

In the Item Returnable Containers page, the items that have been designated as returnable containers are entered and the Quantity (Container) is linked to the quantity of the item. As Fig. 2 illustrates, one Container 500 kg is needed for 500 kg of item P-10600. The bottling quantity can be also lower, for example, 480 kg. Other possibilities can be selected, as seen in Fig. 2.
