Incorporation

Incorporation refers to the additional products that can be added to an existing recipe. The incorporation may already be restricted in the production structure, with a percentage to the complete recipe or by the entry of certain incorporation items that can be used.

: Max. Incorporation % field on production structure PST-034

With the percent restriction on the General FastTab of the Production Structure, users can restrict the maximum incorporation of products.

On the ribbon, click Navigate > Incorporation Items to enter the items where a restriction can occur.

: Incorporation Lines for production structure PST-034

When a production order is created, these specifications are transferred with the production components and calculated in the Incorporation Selection page.

: Components on production order without incorporation item

On the ribbon, click Actions > Incorporation to open the Incorporation Selection page. This page displays each possible incorporation and related inventory information, such as remaining quantities, maximum incorporation percent and already realized incorporation percentages.

: Incorporation Selection for components on production order

To transfer an incorporation item to the recipe, fill in the Incorporation % field and then click OK.

: Components on production order with transferred incorporation item

If re-opened, the Incorporation Selection page displays the quantities of incorporated items that were already selected.

: Re-opened Incorporation Selection page for components

If the full percentage has been selected, no further selection can occur.

: Attempt to incorporate more than allowed percentage
