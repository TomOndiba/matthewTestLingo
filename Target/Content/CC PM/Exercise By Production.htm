Exercise – By-Production

Each time you produce item P-10002, you also get two pieces of item P-954-KP-10002.

Set up item P-10002 to reflect this by-product and then create a production order for item P-10002. Review the routing and component lines.

Create the By-Production for Item P-10002

Follow these steps to create the by-production for item P-10002:

  1. In the navigation pane, click Manufacturing > Product Design > By-Production.
  2. On the ribbon, click New.
  3. Press ENTER or TAB to insert a new number.
  4. In the Item No. field, enter P-10002.
  5. On the Lines FastTab, in the Item No. field, enter P-954-KP-10002.
  6. In the Quantity field, type 2.
  7. In the Status field, enter Certified.
  8. Click OK to close the By-Production page.

Create a Production Order and Verify Setup

Follow these steps to create the production order and verify setup:

  1. In the navigation pane, click Execution > Released Prod. Orders.
  2. On the ribbon, click New.
  3. Press ENTER or TAB to insert a new number.
  4. In the Source No. field, enter P-10002.
  5. In the Quantity field, type 1.
  6. On the ribbon, click Refresh Production Order and then click OK.
  7. Confirm that two lines appear:
    1. One unit of item P-10002
    2. Two units of item P-954-KP-10002
  8. Open the production order component and routing lines for the first production order line (item P-10002) and confirm that each have been calculated properly.
