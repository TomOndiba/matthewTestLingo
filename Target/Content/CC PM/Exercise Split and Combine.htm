Exercise – Split and Combine Production Orders

Create a sales order to customer 10000 (delivery date: February 2, 2019) with the following lines:

  • Item: P-10502, Quantity: 15
  • Item: P-10502, Quantity: 12

Create two new production orders from the Sales Order. Open the Planning Pool page and select and combine the two production orders for item P-10502. Open the Sequence Planning page and split the production order as follows:

  • Machine M023, Quantity 17
  • Machine M022, Quantity 10

Create a Sales Order and Production Orders

Follow these steps to create the sales order and production orders:

  1. In the navigation pane, under Manufacturing, click Planning > Sales Orders.
  2. On the ribbon, click New.
  3. Press ENTER or TAB to insert a new number.
  4. In the Customer field, enter 10000.
  5. In the Requested Delivery Date field, enter 02/02/19.
  6. On the Lines FastTab, in the Type field, enter Item.
  7. In the No. field, enter P-10502.
  8. In the Quantity field, type 15.
  9. On the next line, in the No. field, enter P-10502.
  10. In the Quantity field, type 12.
  11. Select both lines.
  12. On the Lines FastTab, click Order > Create Prod. Order.
  13. Select the Show Production Orders check box and then click Yes to create the production orders.
  14. Click OK to close the message stating that two production orders were created.
  15. In the Production Order List page, click Navigate > Card.
  16. On the Lines FastTab, click Functions > Order Tracking to verify the link to the sales order.
  17. Escape back to the Production Order List page and repeat steps 15-16 for the second line.
  18. Escape back to the Sales Order.
  19. On each sales order line, click Line > Functions > Order Tracking to verify the link to the production orders.
  20. Close the Sales Order.

Combine Production Orders

Follow these steps to combine the production orders:

  1. In the navigation pane, under Manufacturing, click Planning > Planning Pool.
  2. In the General section, enter P-10502 in the Item Filter field to locate the production orders you created for item P-10502.
  3. Select both lines.
  4. On the ribbon, click Actions > Combine Prod. Order Line.
  5. Click Yes to combine the selected production orders.
  6. Click OK to close the message that a new production order was created.
  7. With the new production order line selected, click Navigate > Prod. Order.
  8. On the Lines FastTab, click Functions > Order Tracking to verify the link to the originating sales order.
  9. Escape back to the Planning Pool page and then click OK to close it.

Split Production Order

Follow these steps to split the production order:

  1. On the Planning menu, click Sequence Planning.
  2. In the General section, select the line for work center M023.
  3. Locate and select the production order you created during the combining process.
  4. On the Lines FastTab, click Functions > Split Prod. Order Line.
  5. On the first line, for work center M023, type 17 in the Quantity field.
  6. On the next line, in the Work Center No. field, enter M022.
  7. Ensure that the Quantity field is set to 10.
  8. Click OK.
  9. Select the production order line, click Line > Prod. Order on the Lines FastTab.
  10. On the Lines FastTab, click Functions > Order Tracking to verify the link to the originating sales order.
  11. Escape back to the Sequence Planning page.
  12. In the General section, select the line for work center M022.
  13. Locate and select the new production order for 10 units of item P-10502.
  14. Repeat steps 9-11 to confirm the link to the originating sales order.
  15. Close the Sequence Planning page.
