Exercise – Modify Production Structure to Include Recipes

Change the components on the Production Structure for item P-10002 to calculate the following share percentages:

  • P-30008 = 65%
  • P-30009 = 35%

Select the New Production BOM and New Routing check boxes and then certify the Production Structure. Update the Net Weight on the Item Card for P-10002 to 25 kg.

Manually create a firm planned production order for item P-10002 and check the components. Change the Net Weight on the Item Card to 20, refresh the production order and verify the changes on the components.

Modify the Production Structure

Follow these steps to modify the Production Structure for item P-10002 to include recipes rather than items:

  1. In the navigation pane, click Departments > Manufacturing > Product Design > Production Structures.
  2. Locate and open the production structure for item P-10002.
  3. Change the Status to Under Development.
  4. On the first line, change the Type field to Share of Recipe.
  5. In the No. field, enter P-30008.
  6. In the Share % field, type 65.
  7. On the next line, change the Type field to Share of Recipe.
  8. In the No. field, enter P-30009.
  9. In the Share % field, type 35.
  10. On the General FastTab, select the New Production BOM and New Routing check boxes.
  11. Change the Status to Certified.
  12. In the Production Structure Details FactBox, click Item No. P-10002 to open the Item Card.
  13. On the Replenishment FastTab, type 25 in the Net Weight field.
  14. Click OK to close the Item Card and then click OK to close the Production Structure page.

Create the Production Order and Verify the Setup

Follow these steps to create the production order and verify the results:

  1. In the navigation pane, under Manufacturing, click Execution > Firm Planned Prod. Orders.
  2. On the ribbon, click New.
  3. Press ENTER to insert a new production order number.
  4. In the Source No. field, enter P-10002.
  5. In the Quantity field, type 1.
  6. Expand the Posting FastTab and enter MAINWH in the Location Code field.
  7. On the ribbon, click Refresh Production Order.
  8. In the Refresh Production Order batch job, click OK.
  9. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Components.

: Prod. Order Components page with planned recipe weights

The total Quantity per of the two items equals 25 and each is calculated at the correct percentage as entered on the Production Structure.

  1. Close the Prod. Order Components page.
  2. In the Source No. field, click the arrow and then click Advanced to open the Item List.
  3. With item P-10002 selected, click Edit on the ribbon.
  4. On the Replenishment FastTab, change the Net Weight and Recipe Weight fields to 20.
  5. Click OK to close the Item Card and then click OK to close the Item List page.
  6. On the ribbon, click Refresh Production Order.
  7. In the Refresh Production Order batch job, click OK.
  8. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Components.

: Prod. Order Components page with re-planned recipe weights

The total Quantity per of the two items equals 20 and each is calculated at the correct percentage as entered on the Production Structure.

  1. Close the Prod. Order Components and Firm Planned Prod. Order pages.
