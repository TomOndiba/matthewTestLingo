Sample Shipment

The Sample Shipment functionality provides the ability to ship samples to a customer before the finished item has been created in the system.

Development and laboratory structures are created without an item. In each of these production structures, samples of the product can be shipped to the customer with a sample shipping document without setting up an item.

Note

A Sample Shipment is not a shipment for which an inventory change is posted; it will neither be opened from nor connected to any standard Microsoft Dynamics NAV shipment functions.

For a production structure to ship samples, the Sample Shipment check box must be selected on the General FastTab.

: Laboratory Structure set up for sample shipping

On the Navigate tab, click Sample Shipment. In this page, additional shipping information is entered, such as document date, shipment date and contact data.

: Sample shipment from a laboratory structure

Once all the necessary information has been entered, click Print on the ribbon to generate the Sample - Shipment document.
