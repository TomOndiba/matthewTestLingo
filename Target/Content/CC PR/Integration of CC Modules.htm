Integration of COSMO CONSULT Modules

The usage of checklists for BOM configuration capabilities of cc|configured to order, the calculation capabilities of cc|calculation and the document header and footer text capabilities of cc|text module have been integrated into cc|project to provide greater configuration of BOMs, costing visibility in jobs and flexibility in producing documents from Microsoft Dynamics NAV.

  • With cc|configure to order, users have the ability to add BOMs to Job Budget Calculation lines based on a checklist of various configurations. Additionally, construction orders can be created through the procurement channel “Construction Order” from the Planning and Processing view.
  • With cc|calculation, users have the ability to calculate job costs using different calculation schedules with charges to calculating from sales orders to finished production orders in order to compare the different calculations.
  • With cc|configure to order and cc|calculation together, users have the ability to create calculations for Document BOMs from the Job Budget Calculation line.
  • With cc|text module, users have the ability to add more information to jobs which can then be inserted as header and footer texts to be included in sales and purchase documents.
