Create Sales Quotes

In this section, Sales Quotes are created for fixed price orders, invoicing from the Job, and are updated with Optional and Alternative positions and then the Rolling Calculation is reviewed.

Exercise – Create a Sales Quote for Fixed Price Orders

The job lines to be included in the sales quote must be highlighted, and the line(s) that contain the pricing must have a Sales Code assigned.

: Job lines to be included in sales quote

As Fig. 1 illustrates, a fixed price sales quote is to be created for the Total Price line. With Sales Code = SALESORDER, the fixed price quote will be invoiced directly by posting the sales order – it cannot be invoiced from the job.

Create a Sales Quote for a Fixed Price Order

To create a sales quote for a fixed price order, follow these steps:

  1. On the navigation pane, click cc|project > Jobs > Jobs.
  2. Locate and open the job for which the quote is to be created.
  3. On the ribbon, click Budget Calculation.
  4. Select the position(s) for which the quote is to be created.
  5. On the ribbon, click Actions > Sales Quote > Create Sales Quote.
    The following dialog appears.

: Create Sales Quote position message

  1. Select the correct option:
    • Current line – only the selected line is included on the quote
    • Current line + sub-structure – the selected line and related positions/levels are included on the quote
    • All lines – all lines are included on the quote
    • Selected lines – only selected lines are included on the quote
  2. Click OK.
  3. If the Status is not set to Quote already, a message will appear asking if you want to set it to Quote. Click Yes.
  4. If the system is set up to create versions with new quotes, a prompt will display asking if you want to create a new version for the job with a link to the quote. Click Yes or No, depending on your business process.
  5. A message appears, asking if you want to budget all sales positions. Click Yes or No, depending on your business process.

The Sales Quote opens based on the previously selected job position(s).

: Sales Quote for selected Job lines

This quote can be edited and processed as normal.

Note

Changes made on the Sales Quote will not be reflected in the Job; such changes must be made manually.    

Components without a Sales Code are only shown with the respective unit or quantity. For calculation lines without components, only the Description will be transferred.

In the Job Budget Calculation view, the selected lines indicate that a Sales Quote has been generated.

: Job lines updated with sales quote acknowledgment

Note

Sales Orders follow similar steps; rather than selecting Sales Quote from the Actions tab, select Sales Order.

Exercise – Create a Sales Quote for Invoicing from Job

In the previous exercise, a fixed price sales quote was created that was set up for direct posting from the sales order. This section explains how to create a sales quote that begins the invoice process from the job by using the Job Invoicing Planning and Job Invoicing Release batch jobs.

: New job lines to be invoiced from job

As Fig. 5 illustrates, three lines have been added to the Job Budget Calculation view for invoicing:

  • The Installation line is set up with a fixed price Sales Code (FP), which means that this line cannot be invoiced via the sales order.
  • The Training line is set up with a limited quantity Sales Code (LQ SP), which means that this line is chargeable to a maximum of 16 hours. Fewer hours can be invoiced, but more than 16 hours are not chargeable.
  • The Instruction line is set up with a chargeable as used, time and material Sales Code (USG).

Create a Sales Quote for Invoicing from Job

To create a sales quote for invoicing from the job, follow these steps:

  1. On the navigation pane, click cc|project > Jobs > Jobs.
  2. Locate and open the job for which the quote is to be created.
  3. On the ribbon, click Budget Calculation.
  4. Select the position(s) for which the quote is to be created.
  5. On the ribbon, click Actions > Sales Quote > Create Sales Quote.
  6. A dialog appears; select the correct option:
    • Current line – only the selected line is included on the quote
    • Current line + sub-structure – the selected line and related positions/levels are included on the quote
    • All lines – all lines are included on the quote
    • Selected lines – only selected lines are included on the quote
  7. Click OK.
  8. If the system is set up to create versions with new quotes, a prompt will display asking if you want to create a new version for the job with a link to the quote. Click Yes or No, depending on your business process.
  9. A message appears, asking if you want to budget all sales positions. Click Yes or No, depending on your business process.

The Sales Quote opens based on the previously selected job position(s).

: Sales Quote for selected Job lines

A resulting order cannot be posted directly; the invoicing will be controlled by the “Job Invoicing” function.

Update Sales Quote Positions

After creating a sales quote, and during the negotiation process, it may be necessary to change the calculation to win the order.

Changed job lines can automatically update the attached sales quote line(s) using the “Update” function.

: Quantity changed on Installation position to be updated on attached Sales Quote

Once the line(s) are updated, highlight the lines on the Job Budget Calculation view, and then click Actions > Sales Quote > Update.

In the dialog, click OK and then click Yes to modify the sales line. In the Job Budget Calculation, click Yes in the Sales Quote column for any of the selected lines to view the updated Sales Quote.

: Sales Quote with updated quantity on Installation position

Rolling Calculation after Creating Sales Quotes

Based on the sales quotes created in the previous sections, the Job Rolling Calculation view is updated as seen in Fig. 9. The Job Rolling Calculation view is accessed from the Job Card or Job Budget Calculation view by clicking Rolling Calculation on the ribbon.

: Job Rolling Calculation for Sales Quotes on the Job Budget Calculation view

The three most important amounts from the Job Budget Calculation view are shown in the following fields:

  • Total Direct Cost Budget (LCY)
  • Total Cost Budget (LCY)
  • Sales Line Amount Budget (LCY)

If a Quote and/or Order exist on a line, click the Yes value in the Sales Quote or Sales Order fields to open the sales document.
