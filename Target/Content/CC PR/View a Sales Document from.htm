View a Sales Document from the Job

To view a sales document from the job line (position), follow these steps:

  1. On the cc|project menu, click Jobs > Jobs.
  2. Locate and open the job for which the document is to be viewed.
  3. On the ribbon, click Budget Calculation.
  4. The Sales Quote and Sales Order fields will contain a Yes to indicate that a document has been created for this job line (position). Click the Yes to display the document.
  5. Close the sales document.

Sales Quotes and Sales Orders can also be viewed by selecting the job line, and then clicking Navigate > Sales Quote or Sales Order, respectively.
