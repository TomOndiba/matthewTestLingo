Other Setups

This section describes various other setup pages.

Report Selection Jobs

In the Report Selection - Jobs page, enter the reports to be printed for the various job-related documents.

: Report Selection - Jobs

Job Travel Codes

Job Travel Codes are used for managing travel expenses (refer to topic “General Traveling Expenses” on page 1 for more information).

: Job Travel Codes

Guarantee Types

The Guarantee Types can be used to structure Guarantees which are managed for Vendors, Customers or Jobs.

: Guarantee Types

Completion Reasons

A Completion Reason must be assigned to the Job Card before the status of the Job can be switched to Completed.

: Completion Reasons
