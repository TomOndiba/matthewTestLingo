Job Groups

Job Groups provide the ability to create a grouping of jobs where it is possible to view cost, price, profit and hour information in one screen. Additionally, a calculation can be created for the complete Job Group (refer to topic “Job Group Calculation” on page 1).

Job Groups are accessed on the Departments menu by clicking cc|project > Periodic Activities. In the Job Groups page, press ENTER to populate the Code field and then enter a short description.

: Job Groups

In the Filter field, click the AssistEdit button to open the Job Group Assignment page, where filters are set on the jobs. Set a filter on any of the fields to select jobs to be included in the Job Group, for example, jobs to a particular customer or Main Job No., or multiple combinations of filters such as a particular Main Job No. of a particular customer.

: Job Group Assignment set to filter on Job No. J000001.*

Click OK to add the filter in the Job Groups page.

: Filter set on Job No. J000001.* for the Job Group

Click Apply Filter to apply the filter to the Job Groups page and update the Lines FastTab with the filtered jobs.

: Jobs with Job No. J000001.* added to the Job Group

Jobs can be added and removed with the standard “New Line” and “Delete Line” functions.

The information displayed in the Lines FastTab is the same as displayed in the WIP and Recognition FastTab (budget totals) and Controlling FastTab (all others) of the Job Card; refer to topics “WIP and Recognition FastTab” on page 1 and “Controlling FastTab” on page 1 for field definitions. As with the fields on the Controlling FastTab of the Job Card, all values (except budget totals) can be clicked to access the job entries included in the total.
