Signal Monitor

The Signal Monitor is based on the value in the Realized % Based on cost field and provides two levels of information:

  • A traffic light for classifying the relative cost in the Job Planning and Controlling view.
  • A page for presenting the underling criteria.

The Signal Monitor can have one of three color-coded statuses for positions which have already been started:

Status

Description

l (Green)

The cost is within the budgeted range in both absolute and relative terms.

l (Yellow)

The position is still below the budgeted costs in absolute terms, but above them in relative terms.

l (Red)

More costs have been realized on the position than had been budgeted.

Additional information can be viewed in the Signal Monitor page by clicking Signal Monitor on the ribbon.

: Signal Monitor page for WBS position 1.1.2., Consulting

The Signal Monitor page provides the following information:

  • On the Budget Calculation and Realized FastTabs, the budget calculation fields and the information in the Realized % Based on cost field, the Potential Usage (LCY) field is calculated as a relative amount.
  • On the Categorie Green/Yellow FastTab, the Total Dir. Cost Usage RU (LCY) and Potential Usage (LCY) fields are compared to detect benefits or deficits.
  • On the Categorie Red FastTab, the line is over budget when the value in the Total Dir. Cost Usage RU (LCY) field is greater than the calculated value.
