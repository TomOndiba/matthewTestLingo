Job Shipment

Job Shipments are displayed in the Job Planning and Processing view. All functions described below are executed or accessed from this view.

Shipment Planning

The fields relevant for WBS positions that are to be delivered to the customer are:

  • Shipment Channel – specifies the shipment process.
  • Shipment Date – defines the shipment due date. If no date is entered, cc|project assumes the current date.

To create a delivery schedule for the Conveyor System – as illustrated in Fig. 1 – the Shipment Plan check box was selected on WBS position 1.2. This resulted in an automated planning for this position (as indicated in the Shipment Planning field) and the Shipment Date was taken from the process planning.

: Job Planning and Processing view with Shipment fields

The shipment lines are then displayed in the Shipment Planning view, accessed from the navigation pane by clicking cc|project > Planning > Shipment Planning. On the ribbon, click Planning and Processing to display the complete structure levels (including the lower levels).

Now the complete structure of the shipment can be planned. On the Job Planning and Processing view, select the Conveyor System line, then click Navigate > Shipment > Planning (in the Processing action group) and then select Current line + sub-structure.

: Complete Conveyor System planned for shipment

The automatic Planning Process is based on the entry in the Shipment Channel field:

  • Structure Position – creates a shipment plan without a quantity. 
  • Job Line – creates a shipment plan with the calculated quantity. 
  • Posted Item – creates a shipment plan only if an item was posted through a purchase or inventory request on the job that has not been delivered so far.

All shipment plans belonging to a structure can be viewed and edited together in the Job Processing Shipment Plan view, accessed from the Navigate tab by clicking Shipment > Per Block (in the Views action group).

: All shipment plans for Conveyor System

Job Shipment Planning View

The shipment planning for all job lines can be viewed in the Job Shipment Planning page, accessed from the cc|project menu by clicking Planning > Shipment Planning.

: Job Shipment Planning showing shipment lines in the second quarter of 2019

The fields in the General and Date FastTabs are used to filter the view. The actions on the ribbon allow for the accessing of Job Cards, Job Planning and Processing view as well as the ability to Release selected shipment lines.

Shipment Release

Planned shipment WBS positions (Shipment Status = Planning) can be released by clicking Navigate > Shipment > Release (in the Processing action group).

Shipment documents can be created during the release process. The created documents for the lines are indicated in the Job Shipment field on the Job Planning and Processing view.

: Job Planning and Processing view with released shipment lines and documents

To open the Job Shipment document, click the value in the Job Shipment field or click Navigate > Shipment > Job Shipment (in the Views action group).

: Job Shipment for Conveyor System

The shipment can be arranged in multiple partial shipments (e.g., for multiple trucks) by modifying the value in the Qty. to Ship field.

A job shipment must be released as a document and can then be posted.

Posted Job Shipments

Posted Job Shipments are located on the Posted Documents button of the navigation pane.

: Posted Job Shipment

From here, the posted Job Shipment can be viewed and reprinted.
