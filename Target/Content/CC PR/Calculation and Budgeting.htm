Calculation and Budgeting

As stated in topic “Jobs” on page 1, the job structure is created in the Job Budget Calculation view. This view is also used to enter budget information. Budgeting can be set at any of the levels in the hierarchical structure of the job in cc|project. This feature allows line-by-line budget measurement and control.

This section explains the fields and functions on the Job Budget Calculation view, how to enter lines and other functions performed in this view.
