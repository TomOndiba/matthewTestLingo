Order Network

The Order Network displays all documents relating to a job. Depending on from where it is opened, the view can be specific to a:

  • Customer and show all related jobs and documents (cc|project > Processing > Order Network)
  • Job (and job views) and show only documents related to the job (Job Card > Navigate > Job Documents and e.g., Job Budget Calculation > Navigate > Job Documents)
