Sales Documents

cc|project offers integration of functionality between the job, budget and sales processes. In this way, efficiency and accuracy are increased.

Sales Quotes and Sales Orders can be linked to the job in two ways:

  • A Sales Quote or Sales Order can be created based on a job position
  • A Sales Quote or Sales Order can be imported into a job

In this section, Sales Quotes and Sales Orders are created in the Job Budget Calculation view. Alternatively, sales documents can be created in the Job Budget Sales view.

Note

The Opportunities functionality from standard Microsoft Dynamics NAV Sales & Marketing is integrated in the sales cycle of cc|project. From an Opportunity, a job can be created using the Assign Job function. Once ready for sales processing, a Sales Quote or Sales Order can be created based on a job position. Refer to Microsoft Dynamics NAV Sales & Marketing documentation for more information about Opportunities.
