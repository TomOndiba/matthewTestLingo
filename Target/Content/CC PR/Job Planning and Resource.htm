Job Planning and Resource Planning

Following the budgeting phase, and before or after the sales and purchase quoting phase, planning and scheduling for jobs can be initiated in cc|project.

Planning is based on calculated processes, such as services. During this phase, schedules and measurements are defined; most of this set up is done using the Job Planning and Controlling view, and be further defined using the bidirectional interface with Microsoft Project.

In this section, tools available in the Job Planning and Controlling view are described.
