Rebate/Royalty Rates

Rebate/Royalty rates are set up the same as commission rates. Refer to topic “Commission Rates” on page 1 for more details.

Example – Rebate Rates

In this example, a rebate rate was created for the customer commission group REBATE10 and the commission recipient group REBATE.

: Rebate rates for all items

For all REBATE items of the customer commission group code REBATE10 and the rebate recipient group REBATE, once an amount of 100 is sold, the line calculates a rebate of 10 percent of the commissionable amount of the line. The validity date range is for the year 2019.
