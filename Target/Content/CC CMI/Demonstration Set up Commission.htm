Demonstration – Set up Commission Recipient and Assign Rates and Customers

This demonstration provides an overview of how to create a new commission recipient, allocate the commission recipient to customers and create commission rates.

Scenario: Kevin, the sales manager at CRONUS, has employed Mark, a new sales representative. Mark is taking over the customers in Great Britain serviced from the LONDON responsibility center. Kevin instructs Phyllis, the accounting manager, to create a new commission recipient record for Mark, enter the negotiated commission rates and allocate Mark to the customers for whom he’s responsible.

Create a Commission Recipient

Phyllis creates a new commission recipient record for Mark as instructed. Mark should be a member of the STANDARD commission group, but sales line discounts should not decrease his commission.

Follow these steps to create a new commission recipient:

  1. In the navigation pane, click Departments > Sales & Marketing > cc|commission > Commission Recipients.
  2. On the ribbon, click New and then press ENTER to insert a new commission recipient. Number C-0005 is assigned from the number series.
  3. In the Name field, type Mark Colins.
  4. In the Address field, type 44 Haiden Street
  5. In the Post Code field, enter B31 2AL.
  6. On the Invoicing FastTab, enter STANDARD in the Commission Group Code field.
  7. Clear the Include Line Disc. check box.

: Commission Recipient Card for Mark Colins

Specify Commission Rates

Phyllis enters the commission rates negotiated for Mark.

Follow these steps to enter commission rates for a commission recipient:

  1. On the ribbon, click Commission Rates.
  2. In the General section, enter the following filter information:
    • Sales Type Filter = Customer Commission Group
    • Comm. Recipient Type = Commission Recipient
    • Comm. Recipient Code = C-0005
    • Type = All Items
  3. On the first line, enter the following information:
    • Sales Code = RETAIL
    • Commission Type = Percent
    • Commission = 5.50
  4. On the second line, enter the following information:
    • Sales Code = STANDARD
    • Commission Type = Percent
    • Commission = 4.50

: Commission Rates for Mark Colins

  1. Click OK to close the Commission Rates page and then click OK to close the Commission Recipient Card page.

Allocate Commission Recipients to Customers

Phyllis allocates the new commission recipient record to the customers for whom Mark is responsible.

Follow these steps to allocate the commission recipient to customers:

  1. On the Sales & Marketing menu, click Sales > Customers.
  2. Locate customer 50000, Guildford Water Department.
  3. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Commission Recipients.
  4. Delete the existing recipient, C-0002, Adam Green.
  5. In the Commission Recipient No. field, enter C-0005.
  6. Select the Primary Recipient check box.
  7. In the Share % field, type 100.

: Commission recipient Mark Colins assigned to customer 50000

  1. Click OK to close the Customer Comm. Recipient Catalog page.
  2. Locate customer 60000, Blanemark Hifi Shop.
  3. Repeat steps 3–8.
  4. Locate customer 61000, Fairway Sound.
  5. Repeat steps 3–8.
  6. Locate customer 62000, The Device Shop.
  7. Repeat steps 3–8.
