Contributing Functions

The following is a list of the contributing functions in the cc|commission module:

  • Allocation of multiple commission recipients to one customer with identification of the primary recipient.
  • Commission scales for quantities or amounts.
  • Scale commission and validity periods for commission rates.
  • Commission rates in different currencies.
  • Change of setup values possible for each commission recipient, commission group, or each statement.
  • Possibility of manual entry or modification of commission statements.
  • Detailed insight of calculation information for the verification of specific commission lines.
  • Entry of customers as a rebate or commission recipient.
  • Customer grouping to rebate groups.
  • Sales rebate statement analogue to the commission statement.
  • Fixed manual commission rates for sales lines.
  • Calculation of commissions on amount or profit.
