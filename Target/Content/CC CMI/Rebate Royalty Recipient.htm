Rebate/Royalty Recipient

In the Commission Recipient Card page, the recipient of the rebates/royalties can be entered. The recipient must be created with the same name as the customer. As a rebate/royalty recipient, the customer must be created on the Commission Recipient Card.

When a rebate/royalty recipient is created, the settings defined on the General FastTab of the Commission Setup page are copied to the Invoicing FastTab. If a new rebate/royalty Commission Group is created, those settings are copied to this recipient. The settings of the template can be adjusted at any time.
