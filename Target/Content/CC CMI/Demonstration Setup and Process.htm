Demonstration – Set Up and Process Customer Rebates

This demonstration provides an overview of how to use the system for customer rebates. It shows how to combine customers to purchasing associations and how to create scale commission rates and commission statements.

Scenario: Three customers – The Cannon Group PLC, Selangorian Ltd., and Deerfield Graphics Company – decide to incorporate into a purchasing association headed by The Cannon Group. They enter into an agreement with Kevin, the sales manager at CRONUS, that if their total sales per month exceed defined values, they will receive a credit memo. Kevin instructs Phyllis, the accounting manager, to create a new commission recipient record used as rebate recipient for the purchasing association, enter the negotiated rebate rates, and combine the customers into a rebate group.

Susan, the sales order processor, enters new sales orders for customers of the purchasing association.

At the end of the month, Arnie, the accounts receivable administrator, creates the commission statement used as a rebate statement and issues the commission statement for the purchasing association.

Create a Commission Recipient

Phyllis creates a new commission recipient record for the purchasing association headed by The Cannon Group as instructed. The new commission recipient should be a member of the commission group REBATE.

Follow these steps to create the commission recipient:

  1. On the cc|commission menu, click Commission Recipients.
  2. On the ribbon, click New and then press ENTER to insert a new record. Number C-0006 is assigned automatically from the number series.
  3. In the Name field, type The Cannon Group PLC.
  4. In the Address field, type 192 Market Square.
  5. In the Post Code field, enter B27 4KT.
  6. On the Invoicing FastTab, enter REBATE in the Commission Group Code field.

: Commission Recipient Card for The Cannon Group PLC

  1. Do not close the Commission Recipient Card page.

Assign Customers to Purchasing Association

Phyllis assigns the customers of the purchasing association by assigning the new commission recipient which is used as rebate recipient.

Follow these steps to assign the customers to the commission recipient:

  1. On the Commission Recipient Card page, click Navigate > Customers.
  2. In the Customer No. field, enter 10000.
  3. In the Share % field, type 100.
  4. On the next line, enter 20000 in the Customer No. field and 100 in the Share % field.
  5. On the next line, enter 40000 in the Customer No. field and 100 in the Share % field.

: Customers assigned to Commission Recipient C-0006

  1. Click OK to close the Comm. Recipient Customer Catalog page.

Specify Scale Commission Rates

Phyllis enters the scale commission rates negotiated for the purchasing association.

Follow these steps to enter scale commission rates for the commission recipient:

  1. On the Commission Recipients Card for C-0006, click Commission Rates on the Navigate tab.
  2. In the header, set the following filters:
    • Sales Type Filter = All Customers
    • Comm. Recipient Type Filter = Commission Recipient
    • Comm. Recipient Code Filter = C-0006
    • Type Filter = All Items
  3. On the first line, enter the following information:
    • Minimum Amount = 5000
    • Commission Type = Percent
    • Commission = 1
  4. On the second line, enter the following information:
    • Minimum Amount = 7500
    • Commission Type = Percent
    • Commission = 1.25
  5. On the third line, enter the following information:
    • Minimum Amount = 10000
    • Commission Type = Percent
    • Commission = 1.5

: Commission Rates assigned to commission recipient The Cannon Group PLC

Create Manual Commission Statements

At the end of the month, Arnie, the accounts receivable administrator, creates the monthly commission statements for the purchasing association.

Follow these steps to create a manual commission statement:

  1. On the cc|commission menu, click Commission Statements.
  2. On the ribbon, click New and then press ENTER to automatically populate the No. field.
  3. In the Commission Recipient No. field, enter C-0006.
  4. In the Posting Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  5. In the Document Date field, enter 03/31/19.
  6. In the Statement from field, enter 03/01/19.
  7. In the Statement to field, enter 03/31/19.
  8. On the ribbon, click Suggest Commission Statement Lines.
  9. Click OK to close the message that three lines were created.

: Commission Statement for The Cannon Group PLC

  1. On the ribbon, click Statistics to review the total commissionable amount for this commission statement.

: Commission Statistics to review total commissionable amount for The Cannon Group PLC

Because the total commissionable amount is greater than 7,500 but lower then 10,000, the 1.25% commission rate is used for this commission statement.

  1. Close the Comm. Statement Statistics page.

Issue Commission Statement

After Arnie checks the commission statements, he issues and prints the commission statement for the purchasing association to use it as the basis for the credit memo.

Follow these steps to issue and print an issued commission statement:

  1. On the Commission Statement, click Issue on the ribbon.
  2. Click OK to run the Issue Commission Statement batch job.
  3. On the cc|commission menu, click Issued Commission Statements.
  4. Select the line for the Issued Commission Statement created for The Cannon Group PLC.
  5. On the ribbon, click Print.
  6. Click Print to generate a printed document, or click Preview to review the commission statement on the screen.
