Introduction

The purpose of the cc|commission module is the flexible determination and calculation of commissions. There are various calculation schemes and different commission models which can be implemented.

The strength of this module is the flexible definition of commission and calculation schemes, individual scales, discounts and the ability to include item charges.

Commission recipients, customers, and items can be combined into groups that can have different commission rates with different validity periods.
