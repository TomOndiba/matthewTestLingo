Demonstration – Issue Commission Statements

Scenario: After Arnie has checked the commission statement, he issues and prints the commission statement for Mark to use it as the basis for the payment.

Follow these steps to issue and print an issued commission statement:

  1. Open the commission statement for Mark Colins.
  2. On the ribbon, click Issue.
  3. Click OK to run the Issue Commission Statement batch job.
  4. On the cc|commission menu, click Issued Commission Statements.
  5. Double-click the issued commission statement for Mark Colins.

: Issued Commission Statement for Mark Colins

  1. On the ribbon, click Print.
  2. Click Print to generate a printed document, or click Preview to review the report on the screen.

: Printed Commission Statement for Mark Colins
