Commission Recipients

The Commission Recipient Card page is opened from the cc|commission menu by clicking Commission Recipients and then selecting a commission recipient from the list view.

: Commission Recipient Card, General and Communication FastTabs

The General and Communication FastTabs contain basic personal information about the recipient.

When a new Commission Recipient is created, the settings defined on the General FastTab of the Commission Setup page are copied to the Invoicing FastTab. If a new Commission Group is created, those settings are copied to this recipient. The settings of the template can be adjusted at any time.

: Commission Recipient Card, Invoicing FastTab

The Vendor No. field links the cc|commission module to a specific vendor. In this version, this field is for statistical purposes only and can be used as a link to the finance module later on.

The Customers (Primary Recipient) and Customers (Second. Recipient) fields indicate for how many customers the commission recipient is the primary or secondary recipient. The distinction between primary and secondary recipient can be made in the Primary Recipient field on the Customer / Commission Recipient Catalog pages.

Customers can be assigned to a commission recipient on the ribbon by clicking Navigate > Customers.

Commission rates for the commission recipient can be displayed and maintained by clicking Commission Rates on the ribbon.
