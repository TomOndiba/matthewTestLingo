Primary Functions

The following is a list of the primary functions of the cc|commission module:

  • Direct allocation of commission recipients to customers.
  • Commission recipients, customers, and items can be combined using groups.
  • Specification of commission rates for customers, items, commission recipients, and/or their groups.
  • Determination of the commission base: statements by shipping, invoicing or payment receipt.
  • Possibility of consideration of discounts, credit memos, or return orders.
  • Log values from posted sales document lines for items.
  • Commission statements can be created automatically or manually.
  • Calculating the commission's suggestion on definable periods and listing of the result.
  • Issuing of commission statements and supplying of data for verification and confirmation.
