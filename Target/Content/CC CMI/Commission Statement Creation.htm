Commission Statement Creation

Creating the commission statement can be done manually or automatically:

A single commission statement can be created from the cc|commission menu by clicking Commission Statements. Insert a new record and enter a commission recipient in the Commission Recipient No. field.

If the commission statement is selected from the Commission Recipient Card by clicking Commission Statement on the ribbon, the new record will be automatically filled in.

: Commission Statement created automatically from the Commission Recipient Card

General information of the commission recipient and the calculation period is entered on the General FastTab. All settings on the Invoicing FastTab, which were copied over after selecting a commission recipient, can be adjusted.

Lines for this commission statement can be calculated by clicking Suggest Commission Statement Lines on the ribbon.

: Commission Statement with updated dates and highlighted Suggest Commission Statement Lines function

To create multiple commission statements for batch processing, click Create Commission Statements on the ribbon.

: Create Commission Statement request page

In the Create Commission Statement request page, the calculation period can be determined and commission recipients that need to be processed can be filtered.
