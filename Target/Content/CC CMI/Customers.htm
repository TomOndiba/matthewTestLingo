Customers

Several commission-related values are defined on the Customer Card. On the Invoicing FastTab, Customer Commission Groups are assigned to customers.

: Customer Card, Invoicing FastTab field and Navigate tab menu items

The Sales group on the Navigate tab contains the two actions relating to commissions. Each is described in the following table.

Action

Description

Commission Recipients

Displays the commission recipients that are assigned to the customer. Recipients can be added and modified in this page.

Commission Rates

Displays the commission rates for the selected customer commission group. Rates can be modified in this page.
