Commission Groups

The purpose of commission groups is to group similar commission rates in a business. With commission groups, the system distinguishes between commission groups, customer commission groups, and item commission groups.

Commission groups are directly assigned to commission recipients. A commission recipient is assigned to a unique commission group (refer to topic “Commission Recipients” on page 1).

The Commission Groups page is opened from the cc|commission menu by clicking Commission Groups.

The fields on this page are identical with those of the General FastTab of the Commission Setup page and are used as a template for new commission groups. These fields can be adjusted manually for individual commission groups. As a result, the commission statement is more specialized.

: Commission Groups page

The Home and Navigate tabs contain two actions. Each is described in the following table.

Actions

Description

Commission Recipients

Displays an overview of all commission recipients of the selected commission group.

Commission Rates

Displays the commission rates for the selected commission group. Rates can be modified in this page.
