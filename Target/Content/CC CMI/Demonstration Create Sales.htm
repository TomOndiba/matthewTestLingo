Demonstration – Create Sales Document Lines for Items

Scenario: Susan, the sales order processor, enters a new sales order after Mark has visited the customer 50000, Guildford Water Department, and made a new order. Since the order can be delivered directly, Susan posts the shipment and the invoice.

Follow these steps to enter and post a sales order:

  1. On the Sales & Marketing menu, click Order Processing > Sales Orders.
  2. On the ribbon, click New and then press ENTER to automatically populate the No. field.
  3. In the Sell-to Customer No. field, enter 50000.
  4. Close the credit limit warning message.
  5. To confirm the correct commission recipient is allocated, follow these steps:
    1. On the ribbon, click Navigate > Commission Recipients.
    2. Confirm that Mark’s commission recipient is assigned.

: Commission recipient Mark Colins assigned to the Sales Order

  1. Click OK to close the Sales Commission Recipients page.
  2. On the first sales line, select Item in the Type field.
  3. In the No. field, enter 1900-S.
  4. In the Location Code field, enter RED.
  5. In the Quantity field, type 15.
  6. In the Line Discount % field, type 15.

: Sales Order that includes commission recipient Mark Colins

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Commission Rates and confirm the rate for Mark Colins.

: Sales Order line that includes commission recipient Mark Colins

  1. Click OK to close the Sales Line Commission Rates page.
  2. On the ribbon, click Home > Post.
  3. With the Ship and Invoice option selected, click OK.
  4. Close the confirmation message without opening the posted sales invoice.
