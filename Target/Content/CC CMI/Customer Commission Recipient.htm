Customer / Commission Recipient Catalog

The pages that are the link between customers and commission recipients are opened in two ways:

  • On the Commission Recipient Card, click Navigate > Customers
  • On the Customer Card, click Navigate > Commission Recipients

: Customer / Commission Recipient Catalog pages opened from the Commission Recipient Card (top) and Customer Card (bottom)

One customer can have multiple customer recipients. A commission recipient can be marked as the primary recipient who only has statistical purpose and no impact on the commission statement. In the Share % field, a percentage of commission for the commission recipient can be defined. The sum of all the shares can be greater than 100. A second commission recipient can receive, for instance, an additional commission of 20%.
