Demonstration – Create Commission Statements

Scenario: At the end of January, Arnie, the accounts receivable administrator, creates the monthly commission statement for Mark Colins. After he creates the commission statement for Mark, Arnie checks the commission statement and verifies the values.

Automatically Create Commission Statements

Follow these steps to create an automated commission statement:

  1. On the cc|commission menu, under Periodic Activities, click Create Commission Statements.
  2. In the Posting Date field, enter 01/31/19.
  3. In the Document Date field, enter 01/31/19.
  4. In the Statement from field, enter 01/01/19.
  5. In the Statement to field, enter 01/31/19.
  6. On the Commission Recipient FastTab, enter C-0005 in the No. field.

: Create Commission Statement request page

  1. Click OK to run the Create Commission Statement batch job.

Verify Commission Lines

Follow these steps to check the commission statement and verifying the values:

  1. On the cc|commission menu, click Commission Statements.
  2. Locate and open the commission statement created for Mark Colins.

: Commission Statement for Mark Colins, based on the sales order processed in the “Create Sales Document Lines for Items” demonstration

  1. On the Lines FastTab, click Line > Calculation Information. Confirm the information, for example, that the line discount entered for the sales order does not increase the commissionable amount.

: Commission Line Information

  1. Close the Commission Line Information page.
